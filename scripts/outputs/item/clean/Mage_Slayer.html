    Mage Slayer[edit]

    Mage Slayer
    Ability
    Passive

    Enemy Units
    Damage

    The wielder's attacks deal damage over time to the target, and reduce the target's spell damage output.
    Damage per Second: 20
    Spell Damage Reduction: 40%
    Duration: 3

    • The debuff from successive attacks on the same target does not stack but refreshes the duration.
    • The attacks first apply the debuff, then their own damage.
    • Applies generic outgoing damage reduction on the affected target. Stacks additively with other sources of generic outgoing damage manipulation.
      • Affects spell damage the affected enemy deals. Does not reduce damage flagged as HP removal.
    • Does not work against wards, buildings, and allied units.
