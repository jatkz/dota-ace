    Additional Information

    [edit]
    • Stacks with itself and with Sentry Wards in a single inventory slot, there is no stack limit. The sell value adapts based on the number of instances in a stack.
      • Wards from multiple players are combined when given by allies.
    • Observer Wards do not provide True Sight.
    • Killing an Observer Ward prints out a chat notification to allies.
    • Observer Ward's vision is considered team vision, and is not bound to the player that placed the ward.
      • This means if that player has their vision limited to themselves, wards placed by them do not grant them vision.

    Bounty Interactions

    [edit]

    Grants Gold bounty to the player that provided the True Sight as follows:

    Gem of True Sight
    • To the player who bought or wield the item (regardless of its original buyer).
    Sentry Ward
    • To the player who bought the wards or Planted the ward (regardless of its original buyer).
    Book of the Dead
    • To the player who owns the Level 3 Necronomicon Warrior.

    Lightning Bolt, Thundergod's Wrath or Rocket Flare

    • To the caster of the ability (regardless of the original hero).

    True Sight, Chronosphere, Kinetic Field or Aether Remnant

    • To the player that kills the ward.

    Plants an invisible and phased Observer Ward at the target location, which grants ground vision around it.
    Cast Animation: 0 + 0
    Cast Range: 500

    Holding the CTRL allows Plant to be cast on an allied hero, sharing an Observer Ward with them, using the item's cast range.

    Gold Bounty Increase per Minute: 4
    Experience Bounty Increase per Minute: 8
    The ward takes a fixed amount of attacks to be destroyed. Creeps take more attacks than heroes, treats Illusions and Creep-Heroes as creeps.
    Hero Hit Count: 2
    Non-Hero Hit Count: 10
    Attacks against Observer Wards cannot miss. Melee units have an increased attack range when attacking Observer Wards.
    Attacker Accuracy: 100%
    Melee Attack Range Bonus: 150
    The player that placed the ward can always attack it with any of their units. However, all their attacks are treated as non-hero attacks, until the ward comes within the 50% deny range.

    Lore

    [edit]
    • Knowing how to craft a ward is an important skill for artificers of magical goods. Rylai showed great talent at crafting wards, and once considered becoming an artificer.[1]
    • Despite this, the flavor text for this item refers to Observer Wards as plants and states that they are "often cultivated by apprentice wizards." This implies that they are grown as opposed to crafted, directly contradicting the White Sentry's description.
    • In DotA, this item's name is pluralized to Observer Wards.
