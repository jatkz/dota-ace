    Quell[edit]

    Quell
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Enemies
    Damage
    Attack / Physical
    Grants the wielder bonus attack damage against non-hero units.
    Attack Damage Bonus: 8
    Quell is less effective if its wielder is a ranged hero.
    Attack Damage Bonus: 4
    • Details
    • Misc.
    • Show All
    • Quell is considered as a conditional attack damage bonus.
      • Although the attack damage bonus values are not displayed in the HUD, it is still considered and directly added to wielder's attack damage.
      • The attack damage bonus values are considered by critical strike, cleave, splash, lifesteal and with certain abilities (e.g. Psi Blades and Moon Glaives)
      • It can be reduced only by flat reductions (e.g. damage block).
      • Since it is considered bonus attack damage, it is not affected by percentage-based attack damage bonuses or reductions.
    • The attack damage bonus adapts immediately depending on the wielder's range type (melee or ranged).
    • Stacks with the Quell of Battle Fury, but not with additional Quelling Blades.
    • Does not work against the following units:
      • Buildings
      • Ward-type units
      • Illusions
      • Spirit Bear
      • Couriers
      • Allied units

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Quelling Blade is usually purchased by melee carries who need to ensure that they can get last hits in the lane.
