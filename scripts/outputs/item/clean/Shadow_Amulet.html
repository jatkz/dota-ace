    • item_invisibility_edge_windwalk
      • Recasting the ability refreshes the buff duration.
      • Does not grant the target phased movement.
      • Invisibility
        • Effective duration of the invisibility is 2.25 seconds, due to the fade time.
        • Picking up, dropping, or selling items does not break the invisibility.
        • During the fade time, casting abilities or items does not break the invisibility.
        • The following orders break the invisibility:
          • After the fade time, upon reaching the cast point of abilities or items.
          • After the fade, upon reaching the attack point of an attack, regardless of whether the attack lands or not.
            • For ranged this is upon projectile launch.
