    Corrosion[edit]

    Corrosion
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Enemies
    The wielder's attacks apply armor reduction and movement speed slow on enemy units.
    Armor Reduction: 3
    Move Speed Slow: 8%
    Duration: 2
    The corrosive attacks also reduce the effectiveness of health restoration on the targets.
    Health Restore Reduction: 16%
    The slow is more effective against ranged enemies.
    Move Speed Slow: 16%
