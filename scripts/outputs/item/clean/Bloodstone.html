    For the Beta memorial gems, see Bloodstone of the Ancestor, Bloodstone of the Precursor, and Bloodstone of the Founder.

    The Bloodstone is an item listed at the Base Shop, under Magical. However, it requires items from the Secret Shop to be assembled.

    Bloodstone
    The Bloodstone's bright ruby color is unmistakable on the battlefield, as the owner seems to have infinite vitality and spirit.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • Spell lifesteal from different sources stacks additively.
    • Multiple instances of spell lifesteal from the same source do not stack.

    Ability[edit]

    • Interrupts the caster's channeling abilities upon cast.
    • Applies a basic dispel on the user upon cast.
    • Increases Bloodstone's spell lifesteal against heroes to 80% and against creeps to 16%.
    • Does not apply the buff if the caster has been affected by it in the last seconds.
      • A status buff icon on the caster shows how much time is left for it to be able to get affected by Bloodpact again.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Heroes who rely heavily on mana-intensive, high-damage spells while being in the heart of every team fight.

    Tips[edit]

    • Bloodpact makes spells with an area of effect restore large amounts of health while fighting multiple targets.
