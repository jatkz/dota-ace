    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
      • If a Boots of Bearing was purchased as well, Boots of Bearing's icon takes priority.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 154 health and 0.7 health regeneration.
      • 84 mana, 0.35 mana regeneration and 0.7% flat base magic resistance.
      • 7 main attack damage to / heroes.
      • 6.3 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • Drum of Endurance is a permanent item, using up all charges does not make it disappear.
      • The sell value of the item does not depend on the remaining charges.
