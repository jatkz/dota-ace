    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
      • If a Guardian Greaves was purchased as well, Guardian Greaves' icon takes priority.

    • Interrupts the caster's channeling abilities upon cast.
    • Affects every allied unit, including illusions, invulnerable units and hidden units.
    • Does not heal units that have been healed by Restore in the last 49 seconds, or Mend in the last 44 seconds.
      • A status buff icon on affected units shows how much time is left for it to be able to get affected by the aforementioned abilities again.
      • The stack limit duration has the following properties:
        • The duration is set upon cast and is always 1 second less than the cooldown.
    modifier_item_mekansm_noheal
    Healing Cooldown: Mekansm Restore and Guardian Greaves Mend will not affect a unit that has recently been healed by Restore or Mend.
