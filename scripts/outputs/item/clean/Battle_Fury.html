    Quell[edit]

    Quell
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Enemies
    Damage
    Attack / Physical
    Grants bonus attack damage against non-hero units.
    Attack Damage Bonus: 10
    The bonus attack damage component is less effective if the wielder's attack range type is ranged.
    Attack Damage Bonus: 5
    • Details
    • Misc.
    • Show All
    • Quell is considered as a conditional attack damage bonus.
      • Although the attack damage bonus values are not displayed in the HUD, it is still considered and directly added to wielder's attack damage.
      • The attack damage bonus values are considered by critical strike, cleave, splash, lifesteal and with certain abilities (e.g. Psi Blades and Moon Glaives)
      • It can be reduced only by flat reductions (e.g. damage block).
      • Since it is considered bonus attack damage, it is not affected by percentage-based attack damage bonuses or reductions.
    • The attack damage bonus adapts immediately depending on the wielder's range type ( melee or ranged).
    • Stacks with the Quell of Quelling Blade, but not with additional Battle Furies.
    • Does not work against the following units:
      • Buildings
      • Ward-type units
      • Illusions
      • Spirit Bear
      • Couriers
      • Allied units

    Cleave[edit]

    Cleave
    ▶️
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Enemies
    Damage
    Spell / Physical
    Deals a percentage of the wielder's attack damage as physical damage in a cone in front of the wielder on each attack.
    Starting Radius: 150
    Distance: 650
    End Radius: 360
    Cleave Damage: 60%
    Deals less damage against non-hero units.
    Cleave Damage: 40%
    Ranged units cannot cleave.
    • Details
    • Interactions
    • Misc.
    • Show All
    • Grants cleave if the affected hero's range type is melee.
      • Deals physical spell damage correspondingly to the hero's attack damage value, and is defined as:
        • EXPR
          CleaveDmg = ΣAtkDmg × f(Cleave)
      • Affected by damage block sources on the enemy individually within the area-of-effect.
      • Has the following damage flags:
        • No-spell-lifesteal
        • No-spell-amplification
      • Stack independently with other cleave sources.
        • The total ability area-of-effect is an isosceles trapezoid in front of the hero.
    ILLUSIONS
    Visual effects and sounds still play when illusions attack.
      • The damage is applied immediately to all valid enemy units within the area-of-effect regardless of the ability visual effects.
      • Does not proc cleave when attacking the following units:
        • Ally units.
      • Does not damage the following units:
        • Couriers
      • Neither proc nor damage the following units:
        • Buildings
        • Ward-type units.
