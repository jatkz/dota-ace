    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
      • If a Bloodthorn was purchased as well, Bloodthorn's icon takes priority.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 120 mana, 0.5 mana regeneration and 1% flat base magic resistance.
      • 10 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 4.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Cooldown Sharing
    The items listed above share their cooldowns;
    Casting one of them puts them all on cooldown.
    • The damage counter accumulates all damage instances dealt to the Soul Burn target within the last 5 seconds.
      • The accumulated damage is based on the actual damage taken, after all damage manipulation is applied.
      • Does not include damage with the HP Removal or the no-reflection flag (e.g. Blade Mail).
      • The damage is summed up into and applied as a single instance at the end of the duration, sourced to the caster of Soul Burn.
    • The accumulated is only applied if the debuff expires naturally, it does not trigger if the debuff gets dispelled.
      • Re-casting Soul Burn on the same target refreshes the duration, but does not reset the already accumulated damage.
