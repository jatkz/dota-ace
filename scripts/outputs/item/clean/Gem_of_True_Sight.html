    Additional Information[edit]

    • Gem of True Sight drops when the wielder dies, unless they have Reincarnation.
    • This item cannot be put into the backpack.
    • This item can also be used by the enemies of the owner.
    • Gem of True Sight does not count towards the owner's net worth if it is on the ground.
      • Unlike Divine Rapier, the item does not change owner when picked up by someone else, meaning that it can only affect the buyer's net worth.
    • Both the Tempest Double and Wukong's Command Soldiers do not copy this item.
