    Butterfly is an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Weapons. However, it can only be completed with items from the Secret Shop.

    Butterfly
    Only the mightiest and most experienced of warriors can wield the Butterfly, but it provides incredible dexterity in combat.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 35 attack speed and 5.83 main armor.
      • 35 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 15.75 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • Evasion uses pseudo-random distribution.
      • Stacks diminishingly with other evasion sources.
      • Increases most heroes survivability against physical attacks by 53.85%.

    Tips[edit]

    • If someone in your team already has evasion like Phantom Assassin or another Butterfly, decide if increasing evasion will be a good idea as the enemy team will generally rush a Monkey King Bar. The more evasion a team has, the more valuable Monkey King Bar gets for the enemy team. However, if the enemy team is greatly suppressed and underfarmed, increasing evasion could be a good decision.
