    This article is about the Aghanim's Blessing dropped by Roshan. For the store-bought version, see Aghanim's Blessing.

    Aghanim's Blessing - Roshan was a Kill Rewarditem which could be dropped by Roshan on his third death onwards when killed in the Dire pit.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • Unlike Aghanim's Shard, multiple Aghanim's Blessings can be acquired, however, only one of them an be consumed.
    • Dropped by Roshan on the ground on his third death and onward, but only when killed on the Dire side.

    Interactions: Self-Illusions
    Illusions copy the modifier on their creation. The upgrades fully work for them.

    Most upgrades work normally for the player that drafted the abilities. For exceptions, see Ability Draft.
