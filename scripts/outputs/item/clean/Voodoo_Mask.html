    Additional Information[edit]

    • Multiple instances of spell lifesteal from the same source do not stack.
    • Spell lifesteal from different sources stacks additively.

    Ability[edit]

    Magic Lifesteal[edit]

    Magic Lifesteal
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    Restores the wielder's health for a portion of damage dealt by magical and physical spell damage.
    Spell Lifesteal: 12%
    The lifesteal value is reduced by a factor of 0.2 against creeps.
    Creep Spell Lifesteal: 2.4%
    • Details
    • Interactions
    • Show All
    • Spell Lifesteal
      • Only works with magical and physical spell damage
      • Does not work with attack damage or any pure damage.
      • Does not work with damage with the HP Removal or the no-reflection flags.
      • Stacks additively with other spell lifesteal sources.
      • Treats creep-heroes as heroes.
      • Does not work on the following units:
        • Allied units
        • Illusions
        • Buildings
        • Ward-type units
        • Couriers
    • ILLUSIONS
      • All ability effects are visible on illusions for camouflaging purposes.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    This item is amazing for survival if your hero's damage output mostly consists of area of effect or damage over time abilities.
