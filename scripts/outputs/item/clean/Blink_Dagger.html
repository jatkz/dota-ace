    Cooldown Sharing
    The items listed above share their cooldowns;
    Casting one of them puts them all on cooldown.
    • Blink utilizes the teleport mechanic, and has the following properties:
      • When targeting a point within 1200 distance, teleports directly onto the targeted point.
      • When targeting a point beyond 1200 distance, teleports 1200 distance towards the targeted point instead.
      • Incoming projectiles are disjointed upon teleporting.
    • Doubletap targets a point in front of the team's Fountain, following the same behavior as above.
      • Locations of the fountains, relative to the center of the map:
      • Radiant Fountain: -7456 / -6938
      • Dire Fountain: 7408 / 6848

    Trivia[edit]

    • This item is based on Kelen's Dagger of Escape from Warcraft 3.
      • In DotA, the item was originally named Kelen's Dagger of Escape. Kelen is a rogue from World of Warcraft.
      • Later on, it was shortened to just Kelen's Dagger, because most plays used it aggressively, rather than to escape.
      • In Dota 2, it was renamed to Blink Dagger, to avoid copyright issues with "Blizzard Entertainment".
    • Riki and Lion are the only heroes who are missing responses for acquiring a Blink Dagger.
    • Axe's cosmetic item, Little Blink Dagger, is based on Blink Dagger.
