    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 110 health and 0.5 health regeneration.
      • 60 mana, 0.25 mana regeneration and 0.5% flat base magic resistance.
      • 5 attack speed and 0.83 main armor.
      • 5 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 6.75 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Fragile support heroes often use Ghost Scepter in the late game as a means staying alive longer against physical damage carries.

    • Bane
    • Crystal Maiden
    • Chen
    • Disruptor
    • Earthshaker
    • Io
    • Keeper of the Light
    • Lina
    • Lion
    • Rubick
    • Shadow Demon
    • Shadow Shaman
    • Skywrath Mage
    • Witch Doctor

    Heroes with powerful nukes who intend to amplify their damage with an Ethereal Blade will benefit from a mid game Ghost Scepter.

    • Mirana
    • Morphling
    • Nyx Assassin
    • Tinker
