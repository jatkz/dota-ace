    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 22 health and 0.1 health regeneration.
      • 12 mana, 0.05 mana regeneration and 0.1% flat base magic resistance.
      • 1 attack speed and 0.17 main armor.
      • 1 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 1.35 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Trivia[edit]

    • This item is based on the Ironwood Branch from Warcraft 3.
      • In DotA, the item is named Ironwood Branch as well. Ironwood is a sturdy type of wood from the Warcraft universe.
      • The name was shortened to just Iron Branch, possibly to avoid copyright issues with "Blizzard Entertainment".
    • The happy little trees in the active ability description is a reference to Bob Ross, a popular painter who often used the phrase on his television program, The Joy of Painting.
    • The planted tree uses the same model as the Sprout trees and is surrounded by the same effects as a tree enchanted with Eyes In The Forest.
    • Typing in GG into the shop search bar will bring up the Iron Branch, in reference to their fan name of GG Branches due to DotA 1 giving them the description of Buying one of these will ensure a good game..
