    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 550 health and 2.5 health regeneration.
      • 25 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 11.25 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Trivia[edit]

    • In DotA, this item is named Messerschmidt's Reaver, which is a reference to a crossbow from Diablo II, Messerschmidt's Reaver.
    • According to the comic Are We Heroes Yet?, this item's full name is Reaver's Axe.[1]
    • Axe's cosmetic item, Reaver (Equipment), is based on Reaver.
      • According to its lore text, the Reaver was forged in volcanic fire.
    • Centaur Warrunner's cosmetic item, Black Reaver, is based on Reaver as well.
    • Reaver is featured in the Dota 2 Gamescom trailer, and is described as an item that grants "the might to overpower any foe".
