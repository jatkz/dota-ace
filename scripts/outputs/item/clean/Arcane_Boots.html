    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
    • Movement speed bonuses from multiple pairs of Boots of Speed-based items do not stack.
    • Stacks with all other movement speed bonus.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Heroes who generally want to stay in lane as long as possible need Arcane Boots to supplement their and their team's mana.

    Nuker heroes that rely heavily on their abilities can use Arcane Boots to replenish their own mana.

    Some heroes need Arcane Boots to stay mobile by using their abilities more often.

    Trivia[edit]

    • Arcane Boots was originally created as Arcane Ring, being built out of Ring of Protection, Energy Booster, and a recipe. Its recipe got changed and its theme reworked into Arcane Boots in the 6.68 gameplay patch.
      • Arcane Ring's appearance is designed after this as well, sharing aesthetics with Arcane Boots.
