    Revenant's Brooch is an item purchasable at the Base Shop, under Weapons. However, it can only be completed with items from the Secret Shop.

    Phantom Critical[edit]

    Phantom Critical
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Enemies
    Damage
    Attack / Magical
    The wielder's attacks have a chance to inflict critical magical damage.
    Proc Chance: 30%
    Critical Damage: 80%
    • Details
    • Interactions
    • Misc.
    • Show All
    • Phantom Critical adds magicalattack damage directly to the attack.
    • Critical strike procs have the following properties:
      • The proc is determined during the unit's attack point.
      • The proc chance of multiple sources stack.
        • However, when multiple critical strike sources proc simutaneously, the higher critical factor takes precedence.
      • Critical Strike uses pseudo-random distribution.
    Interactions: Self-Illusions
    Displays critical strike values before all damage manipulation sources for camouflaging purposes.
    • Increases attack damage by an average of 6%.
