    Additional Information[edit]

    Main Article: Interactions
    • Aether Lens increases the effect range differently per ability.
    • For abilities using projectile or travel speed to a certain distance, the travel speed remains the same while the distance increases unless stated.
    • Certain abilities do not have their effect range increased, despite their cast range being increased.
    • Abilities with Global cast range have their max distance increased instead.
    • All vector targeted abilities have their cast range increased but not their effect range.

    Ability[edit]

    Aethereal Focus[edit]

    Aethereal Focus
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    Increases the wielder's cast range for all targeted abilities and items.
    Cast Range Bonus: 225
    Details
    • Stacks with other cast range increasing items or with ability specific talents.
      • Does not stack with the cast range bonus of other Aether Lenses.
    • Only affects ability cast range.
    • Does not increase the ability's radius or area of effect.
      • Does not increase the cast range of active attack modifiers.
