    Trivia[edit]

    • This item is based on the Healing Salve from Warcraft 3.
    • Prior to version 6.54 in DotA, Healing Salve was known as "Flask of Sapphire Water".
      • The name, along with its icon, was likely changed to help visually differentiate it from Bottle.
