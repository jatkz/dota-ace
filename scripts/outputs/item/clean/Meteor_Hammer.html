    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 132 health and 0.6 health regeneration.
      • 288 mana, 1.2 mana regeneration and 2.4% flat base magic resistance.
      • 6 attack speed and 1 main armor.
      • 6, 6 and 24 main attack damage to all / / heroes respectively.
      • 16.2 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • For Kaya-based items:
      • Multiple sources of spell damage amplification do not stack.
      • Multiple sources of mana regeneration amplification do not stack.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Disablers[edit]

    Heroes with non-standard or long duration disables have extra time to channel Meteor Hammer, allowing the wielder to extend the time the target is disabled.

    Clockwerk
    • Power Cogs trap enemies inside of them and make them face Meteor Hammer without much resistance.
    Batrider
    • In certain circumstances where he doesn't need to drag enemies anywhere, Flaming Lasso provides just enough time to land Meteor Hammer, ideally when the affected enemy has many Sticky Napalm stacks for bonus damage.
    Disruptor
    • The Kinetic Field + Static Storm combo traps enemies while preventing them from interrupting the channel; in return, Meteor Hammer augments Disruptor's initiation with damage and a stun.
    Kunkka
    • X Marks the Spot provides a way to reliably reposition the enemy under the meteor, and can be combined with his other AoE stuns for a large amount of lockdown.
    Naga Siren
    • Ensnare roots an opponent long enough to set up Meteor Hammer.
    • Song of the Siren sets up Meteor Hammer. During the song, start channeling Meteor Hammer, and shift-queue Song of the Siren's sub-spell, giving enemies just a split second of time to cast spells.
      • It is possible to do the combo frame-perfect with the help of a toggle item, like Ring of Basilius. While channeling the item, shift-queue the toggle item three times before shift-queuing the song's sub-spell.
      • Note that this combo is quite obvious when executed, since Meteor Hammer's channeling sound and cast animation are visible and audible to enemies, which gives them more than enough time to prepare for it. Even with a frame-perfect timing, enemies can still cast non-targeted instant-cast spells and items.
    Nyx Assassin
    • Impale, in certain circumstances, has enough disable duration to fully channel Meteor Hammer on a whole group of enemies, made better with items such as Blink Dagger and Aghanim's Scepter.
    Sand King
    • Sand Storm's invisiblity sets up for an easy Meteor Hammer.
    Shadow Demon
    • Disruption sets up Meteor Hammer.
    Underlord
    • Pit of Malice traps enemies in a small area to nuke with Meteor Hammer.
    • Firestorm adds to Meteor Hammer's AoE nuke damage.

    Siegers[edit]

    Heroes with abilities that damage buildings can use Meteor Hammer to quickly bring down structures.

    Bloodseeker
    • Bloodrage increases Meteor Hammer's damage perfect for pushing towers.
    Jakiro
    • Liquid Fire adds to Meteor Hammer's building damage.
    • In addition, Ice Path, when properly timed, has enough disable time on a whole team without status resistance to land perfectly, potentially on Macropyre.
    Pugna
    • Nether Blast adds to Meteor Hammer's building damage.

    Hiders[edit]

    Heroes who hide or go invisible can channel Meteor Hammer safely given enemy heroes lack detection or ground target disables.

    Monkey King
    • Tree Dance lets Monkey King use Meteor Hammer while out of vision.
    Riki
    • Cloak and Dagger allows the item to be channeled while hidden by invisibility, allowing him to stealthily initiate targets or push towers.
    Treant Protector
    • During Nature's Guise, Treant Protector can use Meteor Hammer while hidden in the tree line.
    • Treant can also channel Meteor Hammer while opponents are rooted under Overgrowth.

    Special[edit]

    These heroes can use Meteor Hammer during certain circumstances.

    Phoenix
    • Meteor Hammer can be used during Icarus Dive. This means you can start channeling while near an enemy tower and fly back to safety while the channel completes.

    Tips[edit]

    • Meteor Hammer can be hard to aim, as the long and obvious casting allows most opponents to interrupt or dodge the ability. Use the active in conjunction with disables, or after the target has expended their escape abilities.
    • If you find yourself vulnerable to disables, you want to invest for a Black King Bar or a Linken's Sphere to prevent your channelling from getting interrupted easily.
    • Meteor Hammer can be considered a situational item for teams that require extra siege power, as it deals 375 damage against buildings (about 1/4 of the HP of a Tier 1 tower).
    • Meteor Hammer can be used to quickly clear jungle stacks and creep waves.
