    Additional Information[edit]

    • The Aegis is consumed/disappears when one of the following happens, be it in a player's inventory, or still on the ground where Roshan dropped it:
      • Roshan respawns 8 – 11 minutes after being killed.
      • The Aegis wielder dies and reincarnates, consuming the Aegis.
      • 5 minutes pass since the Aegis was picked up by a hero, 4 minutes in Turbo Mode.
        • This means it is impossible for multiple Aegis to exist in a match.

    Lore[edit]

    According to the characters in the Dota 2 Comic "Are We Heroes Yet" Roshan stole the Aegis of the Immortal from a Shop Keeper whom he killed. The Gods then cursed Roshan to have his life and treasure stolen repeatedly as punishment for his crime.[1]

    Trivia[edit]

    • This item is based on the Ankh of Reincarnation from Warcraft 3.
    • Up until 6.42, Aegis of the Immortal was a purchasable item, with an expensive recipe.
      • It used to reincarnate the wielder up to 3 times before disappearing.
      • It also granted the wielder magic resistance, armor, and damage block.
    • In Greek mythology, the Aegis is a shield carried by Zeus and Athena.
    • When selecting Roshan, this item can be seen in his inventory.
