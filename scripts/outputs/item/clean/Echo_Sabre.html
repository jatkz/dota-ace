    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 330 health and 1.5 health regeneration.
      • 15 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 6.75 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Cooldown Sharing
    The items listed above share their cooldowns;
    Casting one of them puts them all on cooldown.
    • The proccing attack has the following properties:
      • The effect triggers upon reaching the attack point of an attack, regardless of whether the attack lands or not.
      • Triggers against and fully affects any target the wielder attacks.
    • Grants the wielder an attack speed bonus upon triggering, the second attack is not an Instant Attack.
      • The attack speed buff has no set duration. It lasts until the user makes a second attack.
      • The buff is removed upon reaching the attack point of the next attack, regardless of whether it lands or not.
    • Echo Strike triggers on Instant Attacks as well, except the following sources:
      • Sproink
      • Side Gunner
      • Infest
      • Boundless Strike
      • Waveform
      • Stifling Dagger
      • Phase Shift
      • Dream Coil
      • Tree Volley
      • Astral Step
      • Assassinate
      • Echo Slash
      • Kazurai Katana
      • However, the slow debuff is still placed by these.
        • This means if any of these abilities apply instant attacks to multiple units, all hit units get slowed.
    • Does not stack with Falcon Rush
      • When Echo Strike is off cooldown, Falcon Rush does not trigger.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Tips[edit]

    • The best items in combination to increase your damage are those which grant critical hit and armor reduction. A Skull Basher is also good to get a chance to bash from the two quick attacks.
      • You can combine the echo sabre with a Daedalus and Satanic's active to instantly restore your HP.
    • Echo Sabre's ability is effectively a passive 1-hit-only Overpower. This makes it a great item on heroes whose passive abilities rely on attack speed to be effective, similar to how Ursa's Fury Swipes relies on the attack speed of Overpower.
    • The attack speed from the passive proc lasts until a second attack is done. This makes it possible to "store" the massive attack speed bonus for a planned strike on an enemy. One just has to attack a creep to proc the passive ability and immediately cancel the second attack. The stored attack speed can come very handy when ganking enemies which have strong attack speed slows like Enchantress with her Untouchable or Phoenix's Fire Spirits.

    Lore[edit]

    Void Spirit is familiar with the Echo Sabre, having held it once before.[1]
