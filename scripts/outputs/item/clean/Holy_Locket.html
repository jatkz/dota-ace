    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 198 health and 0.9 health regeneration.
      • 108 mana, 0.45 mana regeneration and 0.9% flat base magic resistance.
      • 9 attack speed and 1.5 main armor.
      • 9 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 12.15 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Cooldown Sharing
    The items listed above share their cooldowns;
    Casting one of them puts them all on cooldown.
    • Cast behaviors:
      • ↓↓ Double-tapping automatically targets self.
      • Does not interrupt the wielder's channeling abilities.
      • The caster does not require to face the target ino order to cast it on them
      • Can only be cast on heroes, including illusions and creep-heroes.
    • Charges
      • Gains 1 charge for each ability cast by an enemy.
      • Does not gain charges from the following abilities:
        • Active attack modifiers.
        • / Toggle-able abilities.
        • Other sources listed here.
        • Active abilities that are procced passively (i.e. Quill Spray).
        • Abilities cast out of Invisibility, even if the ast breaks the invisibility.
          • This is due to the vision requirement, the ability cast is registered first, then the invisibility is broken.
    • All Magic Stick-based items have the following properties:
      • With multiple copies within the inventory:
        • If multiple are present, the oldest item gains charges first.
          • If the oldest is at max charges, the next oldest starts gaining charges.
    • Can heal and mana restore up to 340 with max charges.

    Holy Blessing[edit]

    Holy Blessing
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    Grants the wielder outgoing heal amplification, boosting all healing they provide.
    Outgoing Heal Amplification: 15%
    Details
    • Grants outgoing heal amplification.
      • Stacks diminishingly with other sources of heal amplification.
      • Neither grants health regen amplification, lifesteal amplification, nor spell lifesteal amplification.
      • All healing provided by the wielder is amplified, regardless of the target.
    • Multiple instances of Holy Blessing do not stack.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Holy Locket has very high synergy with heroes with healing abilities.
