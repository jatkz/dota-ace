    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 330 health and 1.5 health regeneration.
      • 180 mana, 0.75 mana regeneration and 1.5% flat base magic resistance.
      • 20 attack speed and 3.33 main armor.
      • 15, 20 and 15 main attack damage to all / / heroes respectively.
      • 22.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Cooldown Sharing
    The items listed above share their cooldowns;
    Casting one of them puts them all on cooldown.
    • Cast behaviors:
      • ↓↓ Double-tapping automatically target self.
      • Can be cast on Homing Missile.
      • Cannot be cast on other Ward-type units.
    • modifier_item_forcestaff_active
      • This modifier is used when cast on self or an ally, it pushes the target in their facing direction.
      • Forcefully Moves the target at a speed of 1500.
      • Does not disable the target, they can still attack, cast abilities and items, and turn during it.
        • Does not interrupt channeling abilities of the affected target.
      • The direction is determined upon cast, it does not adapt if the target should turn while being moved.
      • Can move the target over impassable terrain.
      • Destroys trees within a 100 radius around the target.
    • modifier_item_hurricane_pike_active_alternate
      • This modifier is used when cast on an enemy, it pushes the target and the caster away from each other.
      • Forcefully Moves the target and the caster at a speed of 2250.
      • Does not disable the affected units, they can still attack, cast abilities and items, and turn during it.
        • Does not interrupt channeling abilities of the affected target.
      • The direction is determined upon cast, it does not adapt if the units should turn while being moved.
      • Can move the units over impassable terrain.
      • Destroys trees within a 100 radius around the units.
    • modifier_item_hurricane_pike_range
      • This modifier is applied on the caster if the target is an enemy.
      • Orders the caster to attack the target upon getting applied, regardless of auto-attack settings.
      • Grants the caster attack speed, and — if they are ranged — infinite attack range.
      • Uses an attack charge upon performing an attack, regardless of its outcome.
        • For ranged heroes, this is on projectile lauch.
    Interactions: Debuff Immunity
    Cannot be cast on debuff immune enemies, but can be cast on debuff immune allies.
    Interactions: Leash Sources
    Cannot be cast on leashed units. However, if reflected, ignores leash.
    • Can be cast on, but does not move units affected by the following abilities:
      • Flaming Lasso
      • Black Hole
      • Chronosphere
      • Duel
    • Cannot push units through the following temporary fences:
      • Kinetic Field
      • Arena of Blood

    Trivia[edit]

    • Hurricane Pike's icon shows that it is half Force Staff, half Dragon Lance.
    • The name may be from the last volume of Kaze no Stigma where the Hurricane Pike, also known as Kokusen, is a divine artifact that greatly enhances the power of a wind mage.
    • Prior to the July 14, 2017 Patch, Hurricane Pike's active ability, Hurricane Thrust, did not have an official name. The name was taken from the Dota 2 Wiki and officially implemented into Dota 2.
