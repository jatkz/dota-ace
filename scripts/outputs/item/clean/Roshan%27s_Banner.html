    Additional Information[edit]

    • On his second death and onwards, Roshan drops Roshan's Banner as a second item if he was slain in the Radiant pit. In the Dire pit, he drops a Cheese instead.
    • Roshan's Banner is consumed upon use. It does not have a charge counter, and multiple Roshan's Banners do not stack in the inventory.
    • Against Roshan's Banner:
      • Attacks from heroes deal 4 damage.
      • Attacks from creeps deal 1 damage.
    • Unlike the Aegis of the Immortal, Roshan's Banner is not reclaimed by Roshan.
