    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
      • If a Gleipnir was purchased as well, Gleipnir's icon takes priority.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 144 mana, 0.6 mana regeneration and 1.2% flat base magic resistance.
      • 12 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 5.4 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Trivia[edit]

    • The particle effects of Cripple include runes resembling the Elvish scripts from The Lord of the Rings, a novel by J.R.R. Tolkien (specifically the alphabet bee or umbar in Tengwar).
    • The item is sometimes referred to as the "Rod of Aui", after pro player Aui_2000, who popularized buying the item on many heroes.
