    Additional Information[edit]

    • Movement speed bonuses from multiple Wind Laces do not stack.
      • However, Wind Lace stacks with other Wind Lace-based items.
    • Stacks with all other movement speed bonus.

    Trivia[edit]

    • The Wind Lace was first suggested on Playdota in 2010 by RCHLNKI.
    • Wind Lace was inspired by the introduction of Javelin, which became the base for proc based items. Wind Lace aimed to be a base for non-boot movement based items.
    • Wind Lace was called "Wind Lance" in the beta.
