    The Hand of Midas is an item purchasable at the Base Shop, under Accessories.

    Hand of Midas
    Preserved through unknown magical means, the Hand of Midas is a weapon of greed, sacrificing animals to line the owner's pockets.

    Details
    • Upon cast, Transmute instantly kills the target unit, dealing
      EXPR
      100% × CurrentHP damage to kill the targeted creep, triggering most on-damage events.
      • This damage fully ignores any form of damage manipulation.
    • Grants the caster 160 unreliable gold and
      EXPR
      1 × XPBounty.
      • The target's default bounties are not granted. This means that no hero nearby gains the default gold and experience from death.
      • Despite the visual effects, the gold and experience are granted instantly.
      • If targeting a Flagbearer Creep, the aura gold bounty is still granted.

    Recommended Heroes

    [edit]

    These heroes have abilities that synergize and allow them to make good use of Transmute.

    Carry heroes who need to farm for late-game items and benefit from the experience and attack speed for farming and/or fighting often buy an early Hand of Midas.

    Some support heroes who are not very item dependent may choose to build Hand of Midas in order to accumulate gold for bigger late-game items and gain levels at a faster pace.

    Tips

    [edit]
    • Transmute fully replaces the targeted unit's gold bounty, so that the target's default gold bounty is not granted.
      • Because of this, Transmute should always be cast on targets with a low gold bounty.
    • The experience bounty of the target is entirely granted to the caster, it is not shared with allies within the area.
    • The earlier Hand of Midas is purchased, the earlier it will pay itself off during the match and result in an effective net worth gain.
      • Assuming it is always used whenever a charge is ready, Hand of Midas increases GPM by 106.67 .
        • It takes 19 minutes and 30 seconds to pay its 2200 cost off, which is 14 casts.
        • It takes 15 minutes and 00 seconds to pay its 1750 recipe cost off, which is 11 casts.
