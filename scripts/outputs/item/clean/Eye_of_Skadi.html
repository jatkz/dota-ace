    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 770 health and 3.5 health regeneration.
      • 420 mana, 1.75 mana regeneration and 3.5% flat base magic resistance.
      • 35 attack speed and 5.83 main armor.
      • 35 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 47.25 main attack damage to universal heroes.
