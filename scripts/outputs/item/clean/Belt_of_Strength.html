    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 132 health and 0.6 health regeneration.
      • 6 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 2.7 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Trivia[edit]

    • This item is based on the Belt of Giant Strength from Warcraft 3.
      • In DotA, the item is named Belt of Giant Strength as well.
