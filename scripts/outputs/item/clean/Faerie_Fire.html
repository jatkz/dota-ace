    Additional Information[edit]

    • When randoming a hero, the hero starts with a free, unsellable Faerie Fire, except in Turbo Mode.
    • The attack damage from multiple instances fully stacks.
    • Couriers are able to use Faerie Fire.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Faerie Fire is often purchased as a ranged equivalent or cheaper substitute for Quelling Blade. It is generally good on heroes who intend to farm and get the last hits, but start out with low damage.
