    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 480 mana, 2 mana regeneration and 4% flat base magic resistance.
      • 40 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 18 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • The projectile speed bonus only affects attack projectiles.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Great on Intelligence heroes who benefit from the additional spell damage to achieve solo kills who also utilize the attack speed and armor.

    Tips[edit]

    • Attacks from Parasma can reduce magic resistance by 20%, therefore this item is very effective on heroes who have any way of dealing of magical damage as it will boost the damage they will deal: Ancient Apparition, Batrider, Broodmother, Dark Willow, Dawnbreaker, Death Prophet, Disruptor, Enigma, Gyrocopter, Huskar, Jakiro, Lina, Luna, Naga Siren, Outworld Destroyer, Puck, Pugna, Queen of Pain, Razor, Rubick, Shadow Demon, Shadow Fiend, Shadow Shaman, Storm Spirit, Silencer, Tinker, Vengeful Spirit, Venomancer, Void Spirit, Visage, Windranger.

    Trivia[edit]

    • This item's internal name is devestator.
