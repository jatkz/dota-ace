    Additional Information[edit]

    • Movement speed bonuses from multiple pairs of Boots of Speed-based items do not stack, the one with a higher value takes priority.
    • Stacks with all other movement speed bonus.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Heroes who rely on quick attacks or on random procs like critical strike will hit harder with Phase Boots.

    Heroes who chase enemies a lot get a great speed boost from Phase Boots.

    Heroes who have high health pool but have low armor will get a considerable increase in effective HP.
