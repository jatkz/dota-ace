    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 572 health and 2.6 health regeneration.
      • 26 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 11.7 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • For Sange-based items:
      • Multiple sources of slow resistance do not stack.
      • Multiple sources of health restoration amplification do not stack.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Tips[edit]

    • Use Overwhelm to interrupt the enemy heroes' channeling such as Town Portal Scroll or any channeled abilities like Freezing Field.
    • Although sometimes optional, it is best to buy items that grant attack speed which procs Bash more.
    • Since the Bash pierces spell immunity, it is best used when facing heroes with spell immunity (e.g. Juggernaut or Lifestealer). The active ability also has a guaranteed 2-seconds stun that also pierces spell immunity.
    • Be careful when facing heroes with skills/items that applies strong dispel (e.g. Aphotic Shield or Dark Pact), it is best to use abilities with silence or mute to compensate this.
