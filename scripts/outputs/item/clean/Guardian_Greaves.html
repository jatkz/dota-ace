    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
      • If a Mekansm was purchased as well, Guardian Greaves' icon takes priority.
    • With Guardian Aura, the item grants a total of 7 armor to the wielder.
    • Movement speed bonuses from multiple pairs of Boots of Speed-based items do not stack.
    • Stacks with all other movement speed bonus.
