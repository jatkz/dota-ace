    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 220 health and 1 health regeneration.
      • 15 attack speed and 2.5 main armor.
      • 10 and 15 main attack damage to / heroes respectively.
      • 11.25 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • The attack range bonus works for ranged heroes only.
    • Stacks with all other attack range bonuses.
    • Affects the cast range of active attack modifiers as well.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Although not a ranged hero, Meepo can use Dragon Lance as a cheap stat item to give Meepo Clones an early boost.

    Tips[edit]

    • Dragon Lance is great for ranged heroes that suffer from low attack range, like Luna or Templar Assassin. Those heroes' powerful attacks are mainly limited by their attack range, which Dragon Lance can fix.
    • Enchantress can throw out very painful Impetus attacks when combined with Aghanim's Scepter, reaching more than 800 attack range. The strength and attack speed bonus are great for her as well, as she is lacking all of that. It can also be built into Hurricane Pike, which is a very useful and complementary item for her.
