    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 360 mana, 1.5 mana regeneration and 3% flat base magic resistance.
      • 30 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 13.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Trivia[edit]

    • In DotA, this item is named Guinsoo's Scythe of Vyse, as a tribute to one of the developers of DotA, who went to create League of Legends.
    • This item is known by many players (and referred to by several in-game Heroes) as "Sheepstick".
      • This name is based on Hex model used in DotA, which is a sheep.
      • The shop's search bar also accepts sheepstick or just sheep as a search term for Scythe of Vyse.
    • The item's name may be a reference to Vyse, the main character of Skies of Arcadia, who had a scythe in some of his early concept arts.
