    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 44 health and 0.2 health regeneration.
      • 24 mana, 0.1 mana regeneration and 0.2% flat base magic resistance.
      • 5 attack speed and 0.83 main armor.
      • 2, 5 and 2 main attack damage to all / / heroes respectively.
      • 4.05 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Agility carries who need an early game damage boost often buy one or more Wraith Bands, because it provides very high damage per gold spent.
