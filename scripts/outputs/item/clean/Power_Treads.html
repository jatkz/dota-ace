    Additional Information[edit]

    • Movement speed bonuses from multiple pairs of Boots of Speed-based items do not stack, the one with a higher value takes priority.
    • Stacks with all other movement speed bonus.
    • There are three ways to assemble Power Treads. Based on which item is chosen, the boot's initial / / attribute status is different. The status can be switched with Switch Attribute freely after purchase.
      • When listed in quickbuy, the item's assembly components are based on the hero's primary attribute. For universal heroes, it shows the strength version.
      • Disassembling Power Treads returns the items which were originally used to build it. It is not based on the currently selected attribute.

    Item Bonuses[edit]

    Power Treads provide 10 and 4.5 main attack damage for strength / agility / intelligence and universal heroes respectively, along with 45 / 55 movement speed and the following total bonuses:
