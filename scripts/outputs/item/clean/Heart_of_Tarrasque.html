    Heart of Tarrasque is an item listed at the Main Shop, under Armor. However, it can only be completed with items from the Secret Shop.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 880 health and 4 health regeneration.
      • 40 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 18 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • The max-health health regeneration bonus from multiple Hearts of Tarrasque does not stack.
      • Can regenerate up to 84% of the wielder's maximum health in one minute.
      • Can fully regenerate the wielder's health within 71.43 seconds.

    Tips[edit]

    • Heart of Tarrasque is effective against teams with a lot of burst damage.
    • Heart of Tarrasque's strength bonus mostly benefits strength heroes. Agility or Intelligence heroes can consider an Eye of Skadi instead for tankiness.
    • Later in the game it can replace a magic resistance item.
