    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grants the following bonuses:
      • 154/198/242/286/330 health and 0.7/0.9/1.1/1.3/1.5 health regeneration.
      • 84/108/132/156/180 mana, 0.35/0.45/0.55/0.65/0.75 mana regeneration and 0.7/0.9/1.1/1.3/1.5 % magic resistance.
      • 7/9/11/13/15 attack speed bonus and 1.17/1.5/1.83/2.17/2.5 main armor.
      • 7/9/11/13/15 main attack damage to / / heroes and 9.45/12.15/14.85/17.55/20.25 main attack damage to heroes.
    • Dagon can be upgraded by purchasing the recipe again, up to 4 times.
    • Upgrading the item improves its stats and also Energy Burst.
    • The total gold cost for each level is 2850//// .

    • Places a debuff on the target, which applies the damage upon expiring. The debuff lasts seconds.
      • Due to server tick rates, the debuff effectively lasts 0.033 seconds. It is not possible to have a 0-second duration modifier or effect delay.
      • This means the damage can be avoided by being invulnerable or hidden as the debuff expires.
      • This also means that multiple casts during the same server tick do not stack and only refresh the debuff.
    • Energy Burst first applies the damage, the damage as heal, then the spell lifesteal.
    • On level 5, the sound effect additionally plays on the target, using the Ultimates mixgroup.
