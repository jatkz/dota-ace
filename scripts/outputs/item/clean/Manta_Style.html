    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 220 health and 1 health regeneration.
      • 120 mana, 0.5 mana regeneration and 1% flat base magic resistance.
      • 26 attack speed and 4.33 main armor.
      • 10, 26 and 10 main attack damage to all / / heroes respectively.
      • 20.7 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • For Yasha-based items:
      • Multiple sources of movement speed do not stack.
      • Multiple sources of attack speed fully stack.

    • The ability effects are applied as follows:
      1. The wielder is hidden upon cast; this disjoints incoming projectiles.
      2. The 0.1-second split time.
      3. Issues the wielder a stop-command, canceling all queued orders, including orders to player-controlled units.
      4. Applies a basic dispel on the wielder.
      5. Creates the illusions based on the ability's cast position.
