    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 220 health and 1 health regeneration.
      • 10 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 4.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • Bonuses from multiple Eternal Shrouds, including Shroud, do not stack.
    • Stacks multiplicatively with other magic resistance sources.
      • Increases most heroes' magic resistance to 40%.
      • With the magic resistance bonus from this source, every 10 points of intelligence further increases the total magic resistance by 0.8%. [?]

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    This item is best used for tanky Heroes who wish to solve their mana problems/augment their mana reserves and increase their EHP against magical.

    As for its defensive abilities, this item provides a good amount of magic resistance and bonus health, great for augmenting a Hero's durability against magical at most, or to simply solve a deficit in magic resistance, such as Ghost Scepter and Necrophos' Ghost Shroud.

    The best thing about this item is its passive. It gives the benefit of gaining mana proportional to the damage points that you take. This is most effective in the hands of those who are the focal point of enemy damage in team fights, allowing the user's spellcasting to thrive from their enemies' efforts.
