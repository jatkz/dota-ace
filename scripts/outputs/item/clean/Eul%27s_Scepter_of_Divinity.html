    Additional Information

    [edit]
    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
      • If a Wind Waker was purchased as well, Wind Waker's icon takes priority.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 120 mana, 0.5 mana regeneration and 1% flat base magic resistance.
      • 10 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 4.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • Stacks with all other movement speed bonuses.
    • Purges first, then applies Cyclone modifier.

    • Applies a basic dispel and Cyclone effect on the target upon cast, turning it invulnerable and fully disabled.

    • The magical spell damage is applied at the end of the duration. If the cyclone gets dispelled, it still applies the damage as soon as the target lands.
      • The damage is only applied to enemy targets.

    Recommended Heroes

    [edit]
    • The scepter's name (and flavor text) is a reference to Eul, the creator of the first DotA map back in 2002.
    • Crystal Maiden's cosmetic item, Eul's Scepter of Divinity, is based on Eul's Scepter of Divinity and shares its name.
