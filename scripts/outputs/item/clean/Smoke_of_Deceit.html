    Ability[edit]

    Disguise[edit]

    Disguise
    ▶️
    Ability
    No
    Affects
    Allied Units
    Turns the wielder and all allied player-controlled units within the radius invisible, with True Sight immunity, upon cast.

    This effect can only be applied once to an allied unit per item per cast.
    Cast Animation: 0 + 0
    Application Radius: 1200
    Dispel Radius: 1025
    Fade Time: 0
    Move Speed Bonus: 15%
    Duration: 45
    While still disguised, any allied unit that comes within proximity of the caster also gets the buff applied to them.
    Proximity Radius: 300
    Couriers can cast Disguise as well, but are not affected by it.
    Casting this ability puts all affected allied heroes' Smoke of Deceit on a fixed downtime.

    Can be reset with cooldown reset sources, but it is not affected by percentage-based cooldown reductions.
    Downtime: 2

    Top Bar Item Indicators for Spectators

    This is visible to spectators-only, indicating heroes affected by Disguise.
    Has the following priorities among top bar items indicators.
    • smoke_of_deceit has three main components:
        • Grants the following ability effects:
          • Invisibility with True Sight immunity.
          • Bonus movement speed.
          • Does not grant phase movement.
          • The affected allied heroes can pick up or drop items without breaking invisibility.
            • The invisibility breaks upon attack point, or the abilities' cast point.
        • Only the caster can share the Disguise cast to allied units within the proximity radius.
        • Interrupts the wielder's channeling abilities and other orders upon cast.
          • Does not interrupt regular move orders.
        • Does not affect the following units:
          • Lane Creeps.
          • Couriers.
          • Hidden units.
          • Invulnerable units.
        • Affected allied units do not provide vision from shared vision sources, unless they are explicitly affected by an offensive expose source that reveals unit models.
      • The invisibility effect is not applied or gets dispelled in the following conditions:
        • Upon entering the elevated fountain area of the other faction.
          • When enemy heroes are present within the dispel radius, regardless of their status effects.
          • Explicitly considers Meepo Clones as enemy heroes, other units as creeps.
          • While other types of clones do not dispel an already placed invisibility buff, they still prevent it from being applied.
    modifier_smoke_of_deceit
    Smoke of Deceit: Invisible, moving x% faster, and hidden from the minimap. Attacking or moving within x range of an enemy hero or tower will break the invisibility.

    Trivia[edit]

    • The item was suggested in the Playdota forums by drgaliza on Dec 18, 2010. It was implemented by Icefrog in the Christmas update.
