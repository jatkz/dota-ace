    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
      • If an Urn of Shadows was purchased as well, Spirit Vessel's icon takes priority.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 220 health and 1 health regeneration.
      • 120 mana, 0.5 mana regeneration and 1% flat base magic resistance.
      • 10 attack speed and 1.67 main armor.
      • 10 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 13.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Cooldown Sharing
    The items listed above share their cooldowns;
    Casting one of them puts them all on cooldown.
    • Cast behaviors:
      • ↓↓ Double-tapping automatically targets self.
      • Does not interrupt the wielder's channeling abilities.
      • The caster does not require to face the target ino order to cast it on them
      • Can only be cast on heroes, including illusions and creep-heroes.
    • Charges
      • Gains charge for each enemy hero that dies within the radius.
      • If the item is at 0 charges, it gains charges at once, otherwise, it gains charge.
      • Does not gain charges from Illusions, Clones, or Creep-Heroes.
      • Does not gain a charge if the enemy hero has Reincarnation.
      • Has to be within the main inventory to gain charges.
    • item_invisibility_edge_windwalk
      • Successive casts on the same target do not stack but refresh the duration instead.
      • Spirit Vessel's modifiers override the modifiers of Urn of Shadows.
    • All Urn of Shadows-based items have the following properties:
      • With multiple copies within the inventory:
        • The one in the higher inventory slot gains charges.
        • The top left inventory slot has the highest priority, the bottom right one the lowest.
      • With multiple copies within the team:
        • The hero that is closer to the dying enemy hero gains the charge.
