    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
    • All of the bonuses from Vladmir's Offering is provided by Vladmir's Aura.

    Ability[edit]

    Vladmir's Aura[edit]

    Vladmir's Aura
    Ability
    Passive / Aura
    Affects
    Allies
    Increases the attack damage, mana regen, and armor of nearby allies, and grants them lifesteal.
    Radius: 1200
    Lifesteal: 20%
    Attack Damage Bonus: 18%
    Mana Regeneration Bonus: 1
    Armor Bonus: 2
    Aura Linger Duration: 0.5
    Lifesteal is less effective when attacking creeps.
    Creep Lifesteal Factor: 0.6
    • Details
    • Status Effects
    • Misc.
    • Show All
    • Multiple instances of Vladmir's Aura do not stack.
      • Fully stacks with other sources of Buckler-based items' armor aura and Ring of Basilius-based items' mana regeneration aura.
    • Lifesteal
      • Only works with physicalattack damage, does not work with magical or pure attack damage.
      • Does not work with physical or any other spell damage.
      • Does not work with instant attacks that do not trigger attack modifier.
      • Stacks additively with other lifesteal sources.
      • Treats illusions and creep-heroes as heroes.
      • Does not work on the following units:
        • Allied units.
        • Buildings
        • Couriers
        • Ward-type units.
    modifier_item_vladmir_aura
    Vladmir's Aura: Gaining x% lifesteal, x% damage, and x mana regen.
    • Grants flat bonus attack damage based on the affected unit's main attack damage. Does not further increase bonus attack damage.
    • Can regenerate up to 60 mana in one minute.
    • Affects invulnerable, but not hidden units.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Carry heroes with low health but high attack speeds benefit more from regeneration, and thus do better with Vladmir's Offering than Mask of Madness.

    • Anti-Mage
    • Broodmother
    • Faceless Void
    • Phantom Assassin
    • Ursa
    • Troll Warlord

    Some melee support heroes can pick up Vladmir's Offering to provide the aura to their teams.

    • Abaddon
    • Bounty Hunter
    • Omniknight

    Trivia[edit]

    • The item's name and theme is a reference to Vlad Dracul III and his fictionalized version, Dracula.
