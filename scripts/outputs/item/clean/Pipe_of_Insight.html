    The Pipe of Insight is an item purchasable at the Main Shop, under Support. However, it can only be completed with items from the Secret Shop.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
    • Stacks multiplicatively with other magic resistance sources.
    • With Insight Aura, the item grants a total of the following bonuses to its wielder:
      • 16.5 health regeneration bonus.
      • Increases the wielder's magic resistance to 44.8%.
      • Every 10 points of intelligence further increases the total magic resistance by 0.736% for the wielder.

    • Interrupts the caster's channeling abilities upon cast.
    • Affects all allied units, including buildings and invulnerable allies.
      • Does not affect wards and hidden units.
    • Grants a magical damage barrier on the affected targets that absorbsmagical damage values, causing several on-damage effects to not trigger within its capacity.
      • Stacks additively with the same damage-type barrier sources, and independently with other damage-type barrier sources.
      • Has a higher priority than universal damage barriers in the group of damage barriers.
      • However, when combined with other damage-negating sources (e.g. Refraction), Barrier does not absorb any damage until the damage-negation sources expire.
    • Successive casts refresh the buff and reset the damage barrier capacity.

    Trivia[edit]

    • Dazzle's cosmetic item Pipe of Dezun is based on the Pipe of Insight.
    • This item's name is based on Khadgar's Pipe of Insight from Warcraft 3.
      • In DotA, the item is named Khadgar's Pipe of Insight as well as well. Khadgar is a character from the Warcraft universe.
      • In Dota 2, it was shortened to just Pipe of Insight, to avoid copyright issues with "Blizzard Entertainment".
