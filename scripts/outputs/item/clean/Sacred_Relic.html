    Additional information[edit]

    • Divine Rapier has a lower priority in item combining compared to Radiance and Nullifier.
      • When having a Demon Edge and a Talisman of Evasion in the inventory, buying Sacred Relic results in a finished Radiance.
      • When having a Demon Edge and a Helm of Iron Will, buying Sacred Relic results in a finished Nullifier.
