    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 110 health and 0.5 health regeneration.
      • 60 mana, 0.25 mana regeneration and 0.5% flat base magic resistance.
      • 5 attack speed and 0.83 main armor.
      • 5 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 6.75 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Tips[edit]

    • Arctic Blast's effect hits every enemy once within its radius, even after its edge has expanded past the target. Because of its slow expansion time (taking about 2 seconds to reach max radius), it can be thought of as a moving area effect, centered on the hero.
      • Use Arctic Blast before blinking or engaging an enemy to instantly deal area damage and movement slow upon initiation.
      • This can be used to instantly disable enemy Blink Daggers by prematurely using Arctic Blast out of vision and then blinking onto an enemy.
    • A useful item against enemies with mana burn. It greatly increases a hero's mana pool, and its aura slows the number of mana burning attacks made against its owner.
    • Good against heroes and creeps with low attack speed, causing them to attack even slower.
