    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 132 health and 0.6 health regeneration.
      • 72 mana, 0.3 mana regeneration and 0.6% flat base magic resistance.
      • 6 attack speed and 1 main armor.
      • 6 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 8.1 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    • Cannot dominate the following units:
      • Creep-heroes
      • Ancient units
      • Siege Creeps
      • Both the Necronomicon Archer and the Necronomicon Warrior.
    • Dominating a creep while already having one dominated instantly kills the previously dominated creep.
    • Applies a basic dispel on the creep upon cast.
    • Manipulates the creep's stats in the following ways:
      • Sets the unit's type to Creep-Hero.
      • Sets base movement speed to 380, regardless of its default movement speed.
      • If max health is below the min health threshold, sets current and max health to 1000.
      • Sets gold bounty to 100 , overwriting its default bounty.
      • Increases base attack damage, it is not granted as an attack damage bonus.
      • Increases health regen and mana regen.
      • Grants an armor bonus, it is not added to the base armor.
      • Does not restore the creep's mana.

    • The number of max allowed units is bound to the player. Buying a new Helm of the Overlord does not allow dominating an additional unit.
      • When owning both, Helm of the Dominator and Helm of the Overlord, Helm of the Overlord takes priority, allowing 70 units.
      • This also goes for the File:Tempest Double icon.png [[Tempest Double|]]'s copied Helm of the Overlord.
      • When the Dominated creep changes faction (i.e. when affected by Holy Persuasion or Enchant), it no longer counts towards the max limit.
      • When the item gets sold or destroyed, the Dominated creep dies.
        • When the item gets sold by a teammate while being disconnected the Dominated creep does not die.
      • The death is not credited to anyone so that no one gets experience from it.
    • Reinforced units receive less damage (before reductions) from the dominated creep.
    • Does not share cooldowns with Helm of the Dominator.
