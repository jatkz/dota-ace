    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 550 health and 2.5 health regeneration.
      • 120 mana, 0.5 mana regeneration and 1% flat base magic resistance.
      • 10 attack speed and 1.67 main armor.
      • 25, 10 and 10 main attack damage to all / / heroes respectively.
      • 20.25 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    • The projectile travels at a speed of 2000.
    • modifier_harpoon_pull
      • Forcefully Moves the target and the caster towards each other for a fixed duration.
      • This results in a possible speed range of 333—3333, based on the pulled distance.
      • Does not disable the pulled units, they can still attack, cast abilities and items, and turn during it.
        • Does not interrupt channeling abilities of the pulled units.
      • The pull destination is determined upon cast, it does not adapt if the target or the caster are moved during it.
      • Can move the units over impassable terrain.
      • Destroys trees within a 100 radius around the pulled units.
      • The pull on the caster and the target is independent, if one of them gets stopped, the other continues being pulled.
    • When cast by ranged heroes, both get moved for 35% of the distance respectively.
    • When cast by melee heroes, both get moved towards the halfway point, meeting at the middle.
    • If cast on a target within 100 distance, the ability does nothing, wasting the mana and cooldown.
    Interactions: Debuff Immunity
    Can be cast on debuff immune enemies, pulling the caster, but not the target.
    • Draw Forth is fully canceled if if the target is Invulnerable or Hidden upon projectile impact.
    • Casting Harpoon instantly breaks Pounce.

    Cooldown Sharing
    The items listed above share their cooldowns;
    Casting one of them puts them all on cooldown.
    • The proccing attack has the following properties:
      • The effect triggers upon reaching the attack point of an attack, regardless of whether the attack lands or not.
      • Triggers against and fully affects any target the wielder attacks.
    • Grants the wielder an attack speed bonus upon triggering, the second attack is not an Instant Attack.
      • The attack speed buff has no set duration. It lasts until the user makes a second attack.
      • The buff is removed upon reaching the attack point of the next attack, regardless of whether it lands or not.
    • Harpoon's Echo Strike uses a custom cooldown system.
      • It is not displayed on the item icon when procced, but instead by a debuff within the status bar.
      • It is not affected by cooldown manipulation and cannot be reset.
    • Echo Strike triggers on Instant Attacks as well, except the following sources:
      • Sproink
      • Side Gunner
      • Infest
      • Boundless Strike
      • Waveform
      • Stifling Dagger
      • Phase Shift
      • Dream Coil
      • Tree Volley
      • Astral Step
      • Assassinate
      • Echo Slash
      • Kazurai Katana
      • However, the slow debuff is still placed by these.
        • This means if any of these abilities apply instant attacks to multiple units, all hit units get slowed.
    • Does not stack with Falcon Rush
      • When Echo Strike is off cooldown, Falcon Rush does not trigger.
