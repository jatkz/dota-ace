    The Assault Cuirass is an item purchasable at the Base Shop, under Armor. However, it can only be completed with items from the Secret Shop.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
    • With the Assault Aura, the item grants a total of 15 armor and 60 attack speed to the wielder.

    Tips[edit]

    • The Assault Aura is a great tool for pushing enemy towers, as it provides reliable attack speed and armor. In addition to that, it also reduces the nearby structure and enemies' armor which can help the nearby allied heroes take them down.
    • The Attack Speed aura works on illusions, but the armor aura does not.
      • The aura is also good for controlled units especially with low base attack time and for the bonus armor and armor reduction of the enemy.
