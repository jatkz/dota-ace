    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
    • Stacks multiplicatively with other magic resistance sources.
      • Increases most heroes' magic resistance to 40%.
      • With the magic resistance bonus from this source, every 10 points of intelligence further increases the total magic resistance by 0.8%. [?]

    Trivia[edit]

    • The game files contain an unused Glimmer Cape modifier, modifier_item_glimmer_cape_building_limit, which gets placed on buildings that were recently affected by Glimmer Cape.
    • The modifier lasts for 180 seconds, is undispelable and prevents buildings from getting targeted by Glimmer Cape, as indicated by an unused HUD error message.
    • This suggests that at one point in development, likely for patch 7.23, buildings were once considered to be eligible targets, just like Couriers and creeps were made targetable by Glimmer Cape.
