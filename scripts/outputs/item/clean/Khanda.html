    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 176 health and 0.8 health regeneration.
      • 96 mana, 0.4 mana regeneration and 0.8% flat base magic resistance.
      • 8 attack speed and 1.33 main armor.
      • 8 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 10.8 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Empower Spell[edit]

    Empower Spell
    ▶️
    Ability

    Self / Enemy Units

    Spell / Magical
    The next unit targeted ability the wielder casts deals additional magical damage in a separate instance, and debuffs the target, slowing their movement speed and breaking their passive abilities.
    Damage: 250
    Move Speed Slow: 30%
    Duration: 4
    Shares cooldowns with Phylactery. If both items are present, the older one takes priority.
    Downtime: 10

    • Affects most offensive unit-targeted ability casts, except the following sources:
      • Item abilities.
      • Death Pact
      • Holy Persuasion
      • Ion Shell
      • Enchant
      • Devour
      • Reflected abilities.
    • Applies Break on the target.
    • If the ability's target turns invulnerable, or if Spell Block triggers, Empower Spell will not trigger.
      • Even if the ability could dispel the invulnerability on the target, Empower Spell will still not trigger.
      • However, if the target turns to invulnerable through the targeted ability, Empower Spell will proc.
    • The order of damage and debuff application depends on the target health when receiving target spell.
      • If the target's health is above 250, Empower Spell's effects get applied before the triggering ability's effects.
      • However, if the target's health below or equal to 250, the triggering ability's effects get before Empower Spell's effects.
      • This behavior ensures that the triggering ability triggers and is not prevented by Empower Spell killing the target in between.
