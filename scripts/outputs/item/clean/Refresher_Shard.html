    Additional Information[edit]

    • On his third death and onwards, Roshan drops the Refresher Shard as a third item if he was slain in the Radiant pit. In the Dire pit, he drops an Aghanim's Blessing - Roshan instead.
    • Although Refresher Shard cannot be bought, it can be sold for 500 . However, only the hero whose main inventory it enters first can sell it.
    • If a Courier is the first unit to pick up the Refresher Shard, it cannot be sold by anyone, even if it remains on the Courier or is given to a hero afterwards.
    • If a Refresher Shard directly enters a backpack when picked up, it remains sellable by anyone until it enters any unit's main inventory.
    • Unlike the Aegis of the Immortal, Refresher Shard is not reclaimed by Roshan.

    Trivia[edit]

    • The Refresher Shard's icon and in-game model are based on Refresher Orb. Its active uses the same sound and visual effect as Refresher Orb.
    • The pop-up that appears when you hover over the item on the ground shows a buying price of 1000 , but when you pick it up, the pop-up shows the selling price of 500 .
    • When selecting Roshan, this item can be seen in his inventory.
