    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 220 health and 1 health regeneration.
      • 10 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 4.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    • Black King Bar is, more often than not, a necessary item on almost all carries to prevent the enemy from simply shutting down their damage output with disables.
    • Initiators can consider a Black King Bar to guarantee a safe initiation against disable-heavy teams. This is especially the case if they can impact the fight with abilities beyond their initiation ability or by providing further damage.
      • Batrider
      • Legion Commander
      • Rubick
      • Slardar
      • Spirit Breaker
    • Heroes with strong channeling abilities can use a Black King Bar to prevent the enemy from interrupting them.
      • Crystal Maiden
      • Enigma
      • Sand King
      • Witch Doctor

    Trivia[edit]

    • Within the pro scene, using Black King Bar before using Town Portal Scroll is sometimes refered to as "Nightfalling".
      • This is a reference to the pro-player Nightfall, who is known for using Black King Bar before teleporting, even if it seemed unnecessary.
