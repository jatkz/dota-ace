      • Interrupts the wielder's channeling abilities upon cast.

        • Reapplying the debuff refreshes the duration.
        • Applies the debuff but does not reveal the following sources:
          • Shadow Dance-based abilities.
          • Smoke of Deceit-based items.
        • Affects the following units:
          • Fogged units.
          • Invulnerable units.
        • Does not affect the following units:
          • Hidden units.
          • Ward-type units.
          • Multiple overlapping Reveal aura radii do not stack.
        • Explicitly applies the movement speed slow on already invisible units.
        • Hence, the debuff does not affect the enemy unit during its fade time or fade delay.
