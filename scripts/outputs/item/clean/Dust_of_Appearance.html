    Trivia[edit]

    • This item is based on the Dust of Appearance from Warcraft 3.
    • Prior to the July 14, 2017 Patch, Dust of Appearance's active ability, Reveal, did not have an official name. The name was taken from the Dota 2 Wiki and officially implemented into Dota 2.
