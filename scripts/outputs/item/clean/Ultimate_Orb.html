    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 330 health and 1.5 health regeneration.
      • 180 mana, 0.75 mana regeneration and 1.5% flat base magic resistance.
      • 15 attack speed and 2.5 main armor.
      • 15 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 20.25 main attack damage to universal heroes.
