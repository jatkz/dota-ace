    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 120 mana, 0.5 mana regeneration and 1% flat base magic resistance.
      • 40 attack speed and 6.67 main armor.
      • 40 and 10 main attack damage to / heroes respectively.
      • 22.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Manabreak[edit]

    Manabreak
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Enemy Units
    Damage
    Attack / Physical
    The wielder's attacks burn their target's mana and deal damage equal to a percentage of the mana burnt.
    Mana Burn per Hit: 40
    Mana Burn to Damage Factor: 1
    Attacks of illusions burn less mana.
    Mana Burn per Hit: 8
    Does not stack with Diffusal Blade-based abilities. Stacks additively with other sources of Mana Break.
    • Details
    • Misc.
    • Show All
    • Mana Break a conditional attack damage bonus and has the following properties:
      • The bonus attack damage is directly added to the wielder's attack damage, but is not visible in the HUD.
      • The bonus depends on how much mana the target actually lost from the attack.
      • Does not interact with the following mechanics:
        • Percentage-based bonus attack damage.
        • Lifesteal.
        • Critical strike.
        • Cleave.
        • Damage block sources.
    • This ability does nothing if the attack target has no mana.
    • Does not work against allied units.
    • The ability effects are applied as follows:
      • The mana removal.
      • The wielder's attack damage.
