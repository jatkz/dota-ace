    • The effects are calculated upon reaching the attack point, not upon landing an attack.
      • For ranged heroes, this is upon projectile launch.
    • Reapplying the debuff refreshes the duration.
    • Does not affect the following units:
      • Buildings

    modifier_item_orb_of_venom_slow
    Orb of Venom: Taking x poison damage per second.
    • The attacks first apply the debuff, then their own attack damage.
    • Deals damage in 1-second intervals, starting 1 second after the debuff is applied, resulting in 3 instances.
      • Can deal up to 30 damage (before reductions).
    • The damage from Poison Attack is direct HP Removal, so it does not trigger any on-damage effects.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Generally picked up by melee heroes who intend to harass their opponents in the lane.
