    Additional Information[edit]

    Main Article: Teleport
    • Alt shows your allied heroes' teleport status and cooldowns under their portraits below the top bar of the HUD.
      • If the ally has no teleport item, no icon appears.
      • The teleport status display has the following priorities: Boots of Travel 2 ➤ Boots of Travel ➤ Town Portal Scroll.
      • The teleport status does not show other teleport abilities (e.g. Teleportation).

    Classified as Teleport Sources
    The aforementioned abilities are considered teleport sources.
    • Acquiring the following items unlocks the subsequent Teleport levels:
      • LEVEL 2
        Boots of Travel
      • LEVEL 3
        Boots of Travel 2
      • Consumes mana, goes into cooldown and does nothing when the channeling is interrupted.
    • Has the following targeting functionalities:
      • Utilizes the min 70-distance for both buildings and outposts respectively.
        • Does not directly target the aforementioned buildings.
        • When point or ground-targeted, the ability automatically searches for the closest valid allied unit to teleport to.
        • Doubletap automatically targets the faction's Fountain upon exceeding the min distance.
        • Requires Boots of Travel-based upgrades to directly target an allied unit, with the following priorities:
          • Buildings Lane Creeps / Summons Allied Heroes
        • When unit-targeted, teleports the wielder directly to the targeted unit's coordinates upon channel completion.
      • The check radius corresponds to point , the targeted building's location.
      • Each building has its own independent check radius.
        • Increases the subsequent teleport channel time as long as the ability is cast on the targeted building, regardless if the teleport is successful or not.
          • EXPR
            ChannelTimen = Teleport1 + Teleport2 + 0.5 × f(n-2)
        • The channel time increase does not apply when teleporting to the Fountain.
        • All ability effects are visible and audible to everyone.
        • For invisible enemy units, the ability effects are visible.
          • While channeling, the teleport destination is marked with the player's color and a sound effect is played.
          • Additionally marks the point-targeted location on the minimap for allied heroes as long as the channel time.
      • Considers Filler Buildings, Barracks and Towers as regular buildings.
        • Grants a 1-second 200-radius flying vision at the teleport destination for the whole channeling duration, regardless if the teleport is successful or not.

    Properties[edit]

    Item Properties
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    All heroes start the game with an unsellable Town Portal Scroll.
    Initial Cooldown: 100
    Grants a charge whenever a hero dies to an enemy or an allied unit.

    The item gained this way neither has gold value nor affects the current Net Worth.

    These Town Portal Scroll are always consumed first upon cast.
    Charge Gain per Death: 1/0/0
    An unsellable Town Portal Scroll in a stack makes every item in the stack unsellable.
    This ability levels up with Upgrade: Town Portal Scroll.
    Details
      • Does not grant charges from the following death sources:
        • Self- denies.
        • Kill credited to neutral creeps.
      • Town Portal Scrolls gained this way does not refund gold when sold upon upgrading to Boots of Travel.
      • Heroes have a dedicated Town Portal Scroll slot on the HUD.
        • The item can neither be droppped nor can be carried in both the inventory or backpack.
      • Has the following interactions with Couriers:
        • Transports the item in their regular inventory.
        • Town Portal Scrolls in their inventory can be dropped and partially share them.
        • Stacks from different owners do not merge in the same slot of the Courier's.
          • Enemy heroes can pick up the dropped item, making them the owner.

    Boots of Travel[edit]

    Upgrade: Town Portal Scroll
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    Upgrades Town Portal Scroll.

    Reduces cooldown and enables unit-targeting allied units.

    The wielder now no longer increases the teleport channel time, and the teleportation no longer consumes a charge.
    Town Portal Scroll Cooldown: 40/40
    All Channel Time Reduction: 0/1
    Automatically and passively refunds all Town Portal Scrolls at full cost in the dedicated slot.
    Cast Animation: 0 + 0
    Set Item Sell-Back Factor: 1
    This ability levels up with Upgrade: Town Portal Scroll.
    • Details
    • Misc.
    • Show All
    • All ability effects are applied once per game.
    • Enables Town Portal Scroll unit-targeting the following allied units:
      • LEVEL 1
        Buildings / Outposts
      • LEVEL 2
        Boots of Travel: Allied non-hero units.
        • Considers clones, illusions, and the Spirit Bear as heroes; other creep-heroes as creeps.
      • LEVEL 3
        Boots of Travel: Allied non-hero units and allied heroes.
    • The entire Town Portal Scroll stack will be refunded at full price as unreliable gold, this includes the following:
      • Purchased Scrolls.
      • The initial Scroll granted during game start.
      • Scrolls granted upon death.
    • Wielding Boots of Travel created by disassembling Boots of Travel 2 also does not repeat the effects.
      • However, upgrading another new item instance does repeat the ability effects.
      • The ability effects are applied as follows:
        1. Instantly sells all current Town Portal Scrolls in the designated slot.
        2. Grants a free Town Portal Scroll.
      • The free item has the following properties:
        • Prevents the wielder from gaining Town Portal Scrolls upon death.
        • Neither has value nor affects Net Worth, regardless of the tooltip value.
        • Affects clones and the Spirit Bear.
