    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 352 health and 1.6 health regeneration.
      • 192 mana, 0.8 mana regeneration and 1.6% flat base magic resistance.
      • 16 main attack damage to / heroes.
      • 14.4 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • For Kaya-based items:
      • Multiple sources of spell damage amplification do not stack.
      • Multiple sources of mana regeneration amplification do not stack.
    • For Sange-based items:
      • Multiple sources of slow resistance do not stack.
      • Multiple sources of health restoration amplification do not stack.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Tips[edit]

    • Much like Sange and Yasha, this is a cost-effective item that greatly increases a spellcaster's mid-game presence.
    • May be purchased if one wishes to start fighting earlier and establish control.
