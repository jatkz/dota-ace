    Overwhelming Blink is an item purchasable at the Base Shop, under Armaments. However, it can only be completed with items from the Secret Shop.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 550 health and 2.5 health regeneration.
      • 25 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 11.25 main attack damage to universal heroes.

    Cooldown Sharing
    The items listed above share their cooldowns;
    Casting one of them puts them all on cooldown.
    • Blink utilizes the teleport mechanic, and has the following properties:
      • When targeting a point within 1200 distance, teleports directly onto the targeted point.
      • When targeting a point beyond 1200 distance, teleports 1200 distance towards the targeted point instead.
      • Incoming projectiles are disjointed upon teleporting.
    • Doubletap targets a point in front of the team's Fountain, following the same behavior as above.
      • Locations of the fountains, relative to the center of the map:
      • Radiant Fountain: -7456 / -6938
      • Dire Fountain: 7408 / 6848
    • The shockwave at the teleport location is instant, despite the visual effect.
      • The shockwave first applies the debuff, then the impact damage.
        • The debuff deals 16.67% of the caster's Strength as damage in 1-second intervals, starting 1 second after the debuff is applied.
