    Additional Information[edit]

    • This item is shown as a Team Item in the shop window when purchased by an ally.
      • If a Pavise was purchased as well, Solar Crest's icon takes priority.
    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 88 health and 0.4 health regeneration.
      • 48 mana, 0.2 mana regeneration and 0.4% flat base magic resistance.
      • 4 attack speed and 0.67 main armor.
      • 4 main attack damage to all / / heroes.
      • 5.4 main attack damage to universal heroes.
    • Movement speed bonuses from multiple Solar Crests fully stack.
    • Stacks with all other movement speed bonuses.
