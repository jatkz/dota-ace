    Blade of Alacrity is an item purchasable at the Base Shop, under Attributes.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • The item's attributes grant the following additional stats:
      • 10 attack speed and 1.67 main armor.
      • 10 main attack damage to heroes.
      • 4.5 main attack damage to universal heroes.
