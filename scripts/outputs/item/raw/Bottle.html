    Bottle is an item purchasable at the Base Shop, under Consumables.

    Additional Information[edit]

    • Bottle containing a rune cannot be shared, dropped, or moved into the backpack.
    • Bottle is a permanent item, using up all charges does not make it disappear.
    • The sell value of the item does not depend on the remaining charges, nor on the stored rune.
    • The Tempest Double does not copy this item, despite not being a consumable.

    Store Rune[edit]

    Store Rune

    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    The item can store a Rune to be used later, or can be passively activated after a set duration.

    Activating the Rune restores all Bottle charges.
    Cast Animation: 0 + 0
    Cast Range: 150
    Max Rune Stored: 1
    Max Store Rune Duration: 90
    Couriers can neither activate nor store runes.
    • Details
    • Interactions
    • Show All
    • This ability is not affected by mute sources.
    • Has the following properties upon cast:
      • Interrupts the wielder's channeling abilities.
      • Breaks invisibility sources.
    • The ability has the following properties:
      • Right-click on a Rune now stores it within the Bottle.
      • Attempting to store another Rune activates the current Rune instead.
      • When the Bottle has a Rune, Regenerate activates the rune, restores the Bottle's charges.
    • The passive activation has the following properties:
      • Passively activated upon reaching its max store rune duration, even upon the wielder's death.
      • Neither interrupts the wielder's channeling abilities, nor breaks invisiblity sources, except Illusion Rune.
    • ILLUSIONS
      The wielder's illusions neither can utilize this ability, nor benefits from it.

    Recommended Heroes[edit]

    Heroes who play in the mid-lane usually do well with a Bottle, because it allows them to control runes.

    Others

    Trivia[edit]

    • Prior to the February 01, 2018 Patch, Bottle's passive ability, Store Rune, did not have an official name. The name was taken from the Dota 2 Wiki and officially implemented into Dota 2.
    • In DotA, this item is called Magical Bottle. The reason for why it was changed to Bottle in Dota 2 is unknown.
