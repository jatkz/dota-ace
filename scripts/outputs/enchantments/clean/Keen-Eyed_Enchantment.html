    Keen-Eyed Enchantment

    From Liquipedia Dota 2 Wiki
    • Item
    • Changelogs

    Keen-Eyed Enchantment is a guaranteed Tier 2–4 neutral item enchantment for Intelligence heroes only.

    ▲
    Keen-Eyed Enchantment
    • Acquired by crafting with Madstone. See the Madstone article for crafting mechanics.
    Bonus ? -10%/-12%/-14% Mana
    +1/1.5/2 Mana Regeneration
    +125/135/145 Cast Range
    Shareable 0
    Usage Alert ? 0
    Recipe


    • This enchantment is derived from the Eye of the Vizier.

    Description
    7.41
    (2026-03-24)
    • Now is a guaranteed option for Intelligence heroes only.
    • Tiers changed from 2/3 to 2-4.
    • Cast Range bonus rescaled from 125/135 to 125/135/145.
    • Mana Regen bonus rescaled from 1/1.5 to 1/1.5/2.
    • Max Mana Penalty increased from 10% to 10/12/14%.
    7.38c
    (2025-03-27)
    • Reduced max mana reduction from 15% to 10%.
