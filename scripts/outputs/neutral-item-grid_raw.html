    Neutral items are craftable items, which require Madstone to be built. Madstone is obtained by farming neutral creeps. They cannot be purchased, and cannot be removed from the dedicated neutral item slot.

    Each player may equip only one neutral item at a time, in a dedicated neutral item slot. Crafting a new neutral item replaces the old one.

    Madstone

    [edit]
    neutral items info menu.
    Neutral items tab in the shop

    Neutral items are crafted with Madstone, which is a resource dropped by neutral creeps.

    Obtaining Madstone

    [edit]
    • Madstone is dropped by neutral creeps upon clearing their camp.
      • If the killing player is within 1200 of the last dying creep, it automatically flies towards them.
      • If the killing player is not within range, but allied heroes are, then it flies towards the closest ally within that range.
      • If an enemy hero is also within 800 of the creep, then the Madstone drops as abundle on the ground instead.
      • If no heroes are within range, it drops on the ground as well.
      • Madstone that dropped on the ground can be picked up by coming within 100 range of it.
    Camp type Regular Turbo
    Killing player Random allies Killing player Random allies
    Regular Camps 2 1 3 2
    Ancient Camps 3 2 4 3
    • If the killing player already reached the current Madstone cap, all the Madstone they pick up gets distributed between their allies.
    • Note that multiple allies get selected always, each getting one Madstone.
    • Casting Transmute on a neutral creep also grants Madstone, as much as clearing a creep camp would do.
    Madstone limits

    The amount of Madstone a player can farm at a time is time-limited. This is to prevent players from crafting higher tier items early on.

    Madstone Cap / player
    (enough for tier)     		Game Time
    Regular Turbo
    0 00:00 – 04:59 00:00 – 02:29
    5 (Tier 1) 05:00 – 14:59 02:30 – 07:29
    15 (Tier 2) 15:00 – 24:59 07:30 – 12:29
    25 (Tier 3) 25:00 – 34:59 12:30 – 17:29
    35 (Tier 4) 35:00 – 59:59 17:30 – 29:59
    45 (Tier 5) 60:00 – 69:59 30:00 – 34:59
    ∞ 70:00+ 35:00+

    Crafting

    [edit]
    The neutral item craft button glows when an item is craftable.
    Colored neutral items are items found by the team
    • Once enough Madstone has been gathered, the player receives an ▶️ audio cue, and the Madstone counter in the HUD starts flashing.
      • The audio repeats upon re-selecting the hero.
    • Clicking on the counter reveals a list of artifacts and enchantments the player can now choose. The list contains 4/5 random artifacts and 4/5 enchantments depending on your hero's primary attribute.
      • On the higher tiers, the artifact list contains the previously chosen artifact + 4 new random artifacts of the current tier, allowing the player to keep the previous one if desired.
      • However, since some enchantments exist in multiple tiers (with scaling bonuses), it is possible to get the same enchantment again.
    • In order to craft a higher tier, the previous tier must be crafted first.
    • Plays an ▶️ audio cue whenever a new neutral item is received.

    These are the Madstone requirements for each tier

    Tier Required Total
    1 6 6
    2 10 16
    3 10 26
    4 10 36
    5 10 46

    Once the Madstone cap is lifted, the player can gather Madstone to re-craft a new tier 5 item. However, the cost of doing so escalates with each re-craft.

    Re-craft count Required Total
    1st 20 66
    2nd 30 96
    3rd 40 136
    4th 50 186
    5th 60 246
    ...
    • There is no limit to re-crafting tier 5 neutral items.

    Trivia

    [edit]
    • The concept of neutral items is likely inspired by the item drop system in Warcraft 3.
      • In this games, neutral creeps within camps drop items when slain, based on level and item category.
      • The original iteration of neutral items in Dota 2 worked very similar to this, with neutral creeps randomly dropping items when slain.

    Token System

    [edit]
    Main Article: Neutral Item Token

    In version 7.33, the Neutral Item Tokens were introduced. Instead of directly dropping a random neutral item of a tier, neutral creeps would now drop a token instead, using the same chances and time-gating as the previous system.

    Activate
    Ability
    No
    Affects
    Self
    Activate to redeem a Neutral Item of its tier.
    Item per Token: 1
    Number of Choices: 5
    Number of Items per Hero: 1
    The Outlanders' arrival brought in more incentives to venture within the neutral jungle.
    Details
    • Upon activation, the wielder can choose out of random neutral items of the token's tier.
      • The token is consumed once the neutral item is chosen, and the neutral item chosen is granted.
    • The neutral item granted is not shareable, and a hero can redeem neutral item per tier.
      • Neutral items claimed are not shareable and are muted when in other players' inventory.
    • Each player has its own selection of neutral items, bound to the player.
      • Neutral tokens cannot be exchanged between players for a different selection of neutral items.
      • However, the tokens are fully shareable and can be sent to the Neutral Stash.
    • Neutral Tokens can be used in the backpack.
      • However, it cannot be equipped in the inventory.
