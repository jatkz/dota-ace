    Bio

    [edit]
    Immortality was overrated. This is much more interesting.
    ▶️  Zeus, the Lord of Heaven
    Alias:
    Lord of Heaven
    Lore:
    Lord of Heaven, father of gods, Zeus treats all the Heroes as if they are his rambunctious, rebellious children. After being caught unnumbered times in the midst of trysts with countless mortal women, his divine wife finally gave him an ultimatum: 'If you love mortals so much, go and become one. If you can prove yourself faithful, then return to me as my immortal husband. Otherwise, go and die among your creatures.' Zeus found her logic (and her magic) irrefutable, and agreed to her plan. He has been on his best behavior ever since, being somewhat fonder of immortality than he is of mortals. But to prove himself worthy of his eternal spouse, he must continue to pursue victory on the field of battle.
    Voice:
    Eric Newsome (Responses)

    Trivia

    [edit]
    • Zeus was the first hero to be implemented in Dota 2.
    • Zeus is based on the Greek mythological god of the same name.
    • As suggested by the game's files, Zeus's name was, at one time, Zuus.
      • The possible reason is that "Zuus" is the correct pronunciation of his name "Zeus".
      • This may also just be an insider joke amongst the developers.
    • Static Field is likely a reference to the Sorceress' skill of the same name in Diablo II.
      • In Diablo II, the spell also reduced enemies' HP by a percentage within a short radius.
    • Zeus' respawn line ▶️ "To err is human. To respawn, divine." is a reference to Alexander Pope, whose quote is "To err is human; to forgive, divine.".
    • Zeus' rivalry line towards Chen, ▶️ "Keep in mind, Chen, godliness is next to deadliness." is a reference to an old and well-known saying "Cleanliness is next to godliness.".
    • His select response ▶️ "In Me I Trust." refers to the national motto of the United States: "In God We Trust".
    • His select response ▶️ "If I didn't exist, it would be necessary to invent me.", is a reference to a similar quote by French philosopher Voltaire: "If God did not exist, it would be necessary to invent him." (French: "Si Dieu n'existait pas, il faudrait l'inventer.").
    • His line upon using Bottle▶️ "Nature abhors an empty bottle.", is a reference to the Plenism postulate attributed to Aristotle, which states that nature contains no vacuums because the denser surrounding material continuum would immediately fill the rarity of an incipient void – "Nature abhors a vacuum.".
    • Zeus' kill response ▶️ "Man proposes, God disposes.", is a reference to a painting by English artist Edwin Landseer with the same name, Man Proposes, God Disposes.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Zeus uses the model of the Mountain King in Avatar form, a Human hero.
      • Arc Lightning is identical to the Far Seer's Chain Lightning.
      • Lightning Bolt is based on the Mountain King's Storm Bolt, with the projectile speed set to 99999, effectively making it instant.
        • The lightning is created by a dummy unit in the sky that casts a 0 damage 0 bounces Chain Lightning on the same target.
        • The same technique is used for the lightnings created by Thundergod's Wrath.
      • In DotA, Zeus' title is "Lord of Olympus".
      • Zeus has a 1/8 chance to spawn in with the name "Merlini", as tribute to the professional DotA player.
