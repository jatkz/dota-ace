    Bio[edit]

    Now that's what I call stopping power.
    ▶️  Kardel Sharpeye, the Sniper
    Lore:
    Kardel Sharpeye was born deep in the mountains of Knollen where, since time immemorial, Keen Folk have survived by hunting the strange, cliff-dwelling steepstalkers above their village, shooting them from a distance and collecting the carcasses where they fell. Sharpeye was among the best of these strange mountain keens for whom projectile weapons are but another appendage, and to shoot is as natural as to touch.

    On his day of summoning, when he was to gain full standing in his village, Sharpeye took the ancient test: a single shot from the valley floor to strike a beast down from the cliffs. To miss was to be dishonored. With his entire village standing vigil, Sharpeye took his shot. A steepstalker fell; the crowd cheered. But when the carcass was collected, the village grew silent, for the elders found that the bullet had pierced its glittering central eye then fallen to be clenched in the steepstalker's mandibles. This ominous sign was the literal opening of a dark prophecy, foretelling both greatness and exile for the gunman who made such a shot. Sharpeye the Sniper was thus, by his own skill, condemned to make his way apart from his people—and unwelcome back among them until he has fulfilled the remainder of the prophecy by attaining legendary stature on a field of battle.

    Gary Schwartz (Responses)

    Assassinate Ability Effects
    sniper_assassinate applies a visual indicator above the unit-targeted enemy.
    • The ability has two main components:
        • Has the following unit-targeting properties:
            • Has a 600-cast range buffer.
            • The unit-targeted enemy has to move at least ≥ 3600-distance from Sniper to be considered out of range.
          • Can target invulnerable units.
          • Does not nullify the ability cast when the unit-targeted enemy is affected by invulnerable sources during its cast point.
          • The following sources applied to the unit-targeted enemy immediately nullifies the ability cast.
            • Hidden sources.
            • Spell immune sources.
        • The ability effects are applied as follows:
          1. sniper_assassinate
            • The debuff applied upon the ability's cast point is visible to everyone, and has the following debuff durations, whichever last longer:
              • A 4-second max duration.
              • For the entire projectile travel distance until upon impact.
              • Until the ability cast is canceled.
              • The crosshair ability effect above the unit-targeted enemy is visible ally-only.
              • Grants the following vision bonuses over the affected enemy unit:
                • True Sight
                • Shared vision.
              • The aformentioned vision bonuses do not apply when neutral creeps are unit-targeted.
              • All vision components are not applied when unit-targeting an invulnerable unit.
            • Applies the magicalspell damage upon projectile impact.
            • Applies the instant attack component upon projectile impact, and procs any attack modifiers and on-cast effects as per normal.
              • Has True Strike.
              • Not affected by disarm sources.
          4. stunned
            • Considers clones and the Spirit Bear as heroes; The Double, illusions, and other creep-heroes as creeps.
            • Resets Assassinate's cooldown if the enemy hero kill is credited to Sniper. This includes the following sources:
              • Reincarnation sources.
                • Killing an allied hero.
                • Self-denying with lethal self-damage sources.
    • SCEPTER NOTES
      Acquiring the ability upgrade immediately update the current projectile.
    Interactions: Debuff Immunity
    Both the True Sight and the shared vision component affects debuff immune units.
    Interactions: Disjoint Sources

    This ability's cast point is not modified when acquired.

      • His rare select response ▶️ "Professionals have standards." quotes a line from the Meet the Sniper video.
      • His kill response ▶️ "I'm polite and efficient." quotes a line from the Meet the Sniper video as well.
      • His rare select response ▶️ "As long as there are two heroes left on the battlefield, someone is gonna want someone dead." quotes Meet the Sniper, too.
      • Incidentally, Sniper's voice actor, Gary Schwartz, also voices Heavy Weapons Guy from Team Fortress 2.
    • Sniper's Assassinate kill response ▶️ "Boom headshot!" is a reference to the Pure Pwnage mockumentary series.
    • His death response ▶️ "Inconceivable!" may be a reference to Vizzini's catchphrase in the film The Princess Bride.
    • His kill response ▶️ "What a dum-dum!" is a pun on the expanding bullet, also known as a dum-dum, a type of bullet designed for low penetration power, but high impact damage, causing large wounds.
    • His last-hit response ▶️ "Oh, a few bullets more!" is a reference to the film For a Few Dollars More.
    • Sniper's name, Kardel Sharpeye, is a reference to the professional Counter-Strike player Kyle "Ksharp" Miller, known for his sniping skills.
      • Sniper's ultimate, ▶️Assassinate, also uses the same, though slightly modified gunshot ▶️ sound as Counter-Strike's AWP.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Sniper's hero concept is originally inspired by and based on the Riflemen, a ranged Human unit.
        • The Riflemen are the only gun-wielding units in Warcraft, with instant ranged attacks.
        • Take Aim's original iteration was based on the Riflemen's Long Rifles upgrade.
        • The Riflemen are Dwarves, a race within the Warcraft universe.
        • Because of this, Sniper's full title is Dwarven Sniper in DotA. It was shortened to just Sniper in Dota 2.
        • In Dota 2, Sniper is part of the Keen folk, along with other heroes that used to be Goblin and Dwarves in DotA.
      • Headshot's original iteration was based on the Mountain King's Bash, with a low stun duration and high damage.
      • Assassinate's original iteration was based on the Mountain King's Storm Bolt, with a high cast time and cast range.
      • Sniper has a 1/10 chance to spawn in with the name "Vasily Zaitsev", referencing a Russian sniper who was active during WW2.
