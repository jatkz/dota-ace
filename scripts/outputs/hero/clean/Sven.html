    Aghanim's[edit]

    Aghanim's Scepter Upgrade:
    Increases cast range, the spell damage, enables casting on invulnerable targets, and transports Sven along the Storm Hammer.

    Applies a basic dispel on all enemy units within the radius upon projectile impact.
    Does not travel with the Storm Hammer with Alt-Cast toggled off.

    The ability deals lesser damage upon projectile impact.
    Aghanim's Shard Upgrade:
    Warcry now passively grants armor to allied units within the radius.

    The ability's active buff can now only be dispelled upon Sven's death, rather than any dispel.

    Bio[edit]

    May my enemies share the fate of the Shattered Helm.
    ▶️  Sven, the Rogue Knight
    Alias:
    Rogue Knight
    Lore:
    Sven is the bastard son of a Vigil Knight, born of a Pallid Meranth, raised in the Shadeshore Ruins. With his father executed for violating the Vigil Codex, and his mother shunned by her wild race, Sven believes that honor is to be found in no social order, but only in himself. After tending his mother through a lingering death, he offered himself as a novice to the Vigil Knights, never revealing his identity. For thirteen years he studied in his father's school, mastering the rigid code that declared his existence an abomination. Then, on the day that should have been his In-Swearing, he seized the Outcast Blade, shattered the Sacred Helm, and burned the Codex in the Vigil's Holy Flame. He strode from Vigil Keep, forever solitary, following his private code to the last strict rune. Still a knight, yes...but a rogue knight. He answers only to himself.
    Voice:
    David Scully (Responses)

    Trivia[edit]

    • Sven is named after Danish bodybuilder and actor Sven-Ole Thorsen.
      • In earlier versions of DotA, Sven had the fun name "Arnold Schwarzenegger".
    • ▶️ "Twas only a flesh wound" and ▶️ "It was but a scratch" are references to the Black Knight from "Monty Python and the Holy Grail".
    • ▶️ "Blame the host" and ▶️ "Bad host" are references to the original "Warcraft III" DotA gameplay when game stability depended on the quality of internet connection on the host player.
    • Storm Hammer was named Storm Bolt in the early beta phases and was renamed into Storm Hammer at one point. It is also its original name in DotA and reflected in one of Sven's cast responses in Dota 2, where he is referring to it as ▶️ "Storm Bolt".
      • Rubick also has the same response when casting an acquired Storm Hammer.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Sven's hero concept is originally inspired by the, Felguard Overlords.
        • Great Cleave is identical to the Felguards' Cleaving Attack.
      • Storm Hammer's original iteration was identical to the Mountain King's Storm Bolt.
        • The area of effect was added much later in 6.60, until then, the abilities were identical.
        • In DotA, it was called Storm Bolt as well. The name was used during the early beta of Dota 2, but got renamed at some point.
        • One of Sven's cast responses for Storm Hammer still refers to it as ▶️ "Storm Bolt".
      • Sven's old ability Toughness Aura was identical to the Paladin's Devotion Aura, a passive aura that grants armor to allies.
      • Warcry's original iteration was identical to the Scroll of Protection, a consumable item that grants armor to allies when used.
      • God's Strength's original iteration was identical to the Druid of the Claw's Roar, but only affecting Sven himself.
        • As such, God's Strength was dispellable, and did not turn Sven red.
        • Later on, the ability was reworked to its current iteration, becoming undispellable and turning Sven red when cast.
        • In DotA, this was achieved through a transformation spell, using the Felguard Bloodfiend model.
