    Bio[edit]

    Purer than flesh, stronger than bone, imperishable is the essence of the wraith.
    ▶️  Ostarion, the Wraith King
    Aliases:
    One True King, Ostarion, SK, SNK, Skeleton, WK
    Lore:
    For untold years, King Ostarion built a kingdom from the remains of his enemies. It was an obsessive's errand, done to pass the long eternities of a monarchy that seemed fated never to end. He believed that as long as he built up the towers of his palace, he could not die. But eventually he learned that he had been deluded... that bone itself could perish. Deeply mistrustful of flesh, he sought a more permanent way of extending his reign, and at last settled on pursuit of wraith energy, a form of pure spirit given off by certain dark souls at death. Should he infuse himself with Wraith Essence, he thought he might create a body as luminous and eternal as his ego. On the millennial solstice known as Wraith-Night, he submitted to a rite of transformation, compelling his subjects to harvest enough souls to fuel his ambition for immortality. No one knows how many of his champions died, for the only survivor who mattered was the Wraith King who rose with the sun on the following morn. Now he rarely spends a moment on his glowing throne--but strides out with sword drawn, demanding a fealty that extends far beyond death.
    Voice:
    Dave Fennoy (Responses)

        • The active component has the following properties:
          • Min health sources have precedence over the active component cast.
          • Doubletap activates the ability.
            • However, when Wraith King is affected by invulnerable sources, he is unable to cast abilities on himself; hence he is unable to utilize doubletap to self-cast.
          • The active cast ability effects are applied as follows:
            1. Reduces Wraith King's mana accordingly to the mana cost.
                • Offensive ability components that apply prior to the ability cast may kill Wraith King upon the active Reincarnation cast.
                • This causes the following mana restore component to not proc, and indirectly causes Reincarnation to not proc due to not having enough current mana.
            2. Self-instant kills.
            3. Self-mana restores based on the ability's mana cost.
            4. Procs the ability's passive component.
          • Reincarnates and grants the following bonuses upon activation:
            • 100% max health and max mana.
              • Does not reset ability cooldowns.
            • Grants a 3-second daytime and nighttimevision bonus based on Wraith King's current vision values at his death location.
          • skeleton_king_reincarnate_slow
            • Applies both the movement speed slow and attack speed slow.
            • TALENT NOTES
              The Wraithfire Blast casttalent replaces the slow component with Wraithfire Blast of its corresponding level.
              • Affects the following units:
                • Fogged units.
                • Invisible units.
          • FACET NOTES
            • All facet-related traits consider clones, illusions, and creep-heroes as heroes.
            • Bone Guard
              • Summons Wraith King Skeletons based on its ability level, per affected enemy hero within the radius.
                • Has the following properties upon proc:
                  • Works independently of Bone Guard's cooldown, and does not cost mana.
                  • Does not proc on-cast effects.
              • Affects the following units:
                • Fogged units.
                • Invisible units.
                • Untargetable units.
              • Does not affect the following units:
                • Hidden units.
                • Invulnerable units.
            • Spectral Blade
              • Removes the skeleton_king_curse_cooldown downtime debuff from all affected heroes.

    Trivia[edit]

    • Prior to December 13 of 2013, "Ostarion, the Wraith King" was known as "Ostarion, the Skeleton King".
      • On December 10 of 2013, Skeleton King was removed from the game for "pressing ceremonial reasons". After the Wraith Night event, he returned as the Wraith King.
      • His model was completely reworked from the previous skeleton model to the current model. His color theme was changed from red to green and all his icons were changed accordingly.
      • He also got a new voice set, by the same voice actor who also voiced Skeleton King previously. Skeleton King's responses can be found "here".
      • For The International 10 Battle Pass, however, Skeleton King would return as an Arcana, which can be acquired by players that reach Level 375.
    • In DotA 1, Wraith King's (who is still known as the Skeleton King in DotA 1) name is "Leoric". This is a reference to the Skeleton King of Diablo who is also named Leoric. In the transition to Dota 2, his name was changed to Ostarion to avoid copyright issues.
    • Copyright issues may also be a reason to why Skeleton King was remodeled and re-titled into Wraith King. Another likely factor for the remodeling was to solve many cosmetic item conflicts with the low-violence model which was made for the Perfect World client. A low-violence model was required for Skeleton King due to strict policies in China against the usage of skeletons in video games.
    • Wraith King's responses for buying Armlet of Mordiggian▶️ "I NOW HAVE ALL THE SEXY", buying a Divine Rapier▶️ "Oh, Rapier. NOW YOUR KING IS BALLING OUT OF CONTROL!" and his battle begin line ▶️ "I'LL HELP MY WEAK AND PUNY ALLIES WHERE I CAN." are a reference to a popular DotA hero guide called "CAPS LOCK LEORIC".
      • When Dota Plus first emerged with the use of a Hero's responses, many community members asked Valve to properly make the text of all of Wraith King's lines in all caps, which was done after multiple requests.
    • In several responses, he refers to himself as the "Once and Future King", which is also a title for the legendary King Arthur.
    • One of Wraith King's lines is "We'll lead as three kings" when he activates an Illusion Rune. This line is a reference to the three kings or three wise men story from Christmas.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Skeleton King uses the model of the Skeleton Warriors, which are summoned Undead units.
        • The model has been scaled up a lot, and a crown was added, making this a custom model.
      • Wraithfire Blast's original iteration was identical to Firebolt, a single target projectile that damages and stuns.
      • Wraith King's old ability, Vampiric Aura, was identical to the Dread Lord's Vampiric Aura, an aura that grants nearby allies lifesteal.
      • Mortal Strike's original iteration was identical to the Blademaster's Critical Strike, gaining a chance to deal multiplied attack damage.
      • Reincarnation's original iteration was identical to the Tauren Chieftain's Reincarnation, respawning shortly after death at the death location.
