    Bio

    [edit]
    Whistle
    ▶️  Marci
    Lore:
    Admired by many, Marci's origins are known to nameless few. She travels mostly in the company of the Princess Mirana, but the roots of their friendship are entwined in secrets neither would ever needlessly reveal. To allies, she serves as fierce and honest companion. To enemies, she acts as dauntless deterrence against harming any she deems friend. Though unwise opponents may consider her size unremarkable, Marci possesses an inner power that imbues her strikes with incredible might. She'll decimate those who think to test her, but those who earn her favor will have an unflappable confidant for life.

    Trivia

    [edit]
    • Marci first debuted in the Dota: Dragon's Blood series and her appearance was designed in cooperation with Studio Mir, the producing studio behind the series.
    • Marci was revealed as a new playable hero during The International 2021.[1]
    • Within the internal game files, Marci's abilities are named differently:
      • Dispose is named Grapple.
      • Rebound is in some instances named Companion Run.
      • Sidekick is in some instances named Guardian.
    • The symbol featured on her Sidekick spell resembles the one emblazoned on the Invoker's Cape of the Dark Magic. It's unknown what this similarity signifies.
    • Marci performs a judo throw known as a tomoe-nage in one of the two animations of her Dispose ability.
