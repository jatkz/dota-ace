    Bio

    [edit]
    I'll paint this world as I see fit.
    ▶️  Grimstroke
    Alias:
    GS
    Lore:
    The people of Ashkavor crowded around the temple square, eager to witness the ascension of their new guardian -- to stand near the man as he bound their souls to his own. But as his final brush strokes fell against the runestone, and the bond of a new Ascended One was forged, everyone -- even those who'd stayed in their homes -- could sense that something had gone terribly wrong.

    He knew the cause instantly. The droplets of ichor he'd procured to amplify the potency of his inkpots had instead contaminated them, and the power of the binding spell he'd cast now threatened to consume him. An inky corruption snaked upward from the runestone, along the handle of his brush, soon overtaking his hands. From there it spread quickly. Once it overcame his face and his mouth, he couldn't have screamed even if he'd wanted to.

    All of his life he'd calculated on how to attain ever greater powers than the limits presented by his teachers would allow--going so far as to break the sacred prohibition against augmenting the inks. Indeed, with the Wellspring of the Ascended Bond open to him, he felt a measure of power coursing into his soul like he'd never imagined. His greatest triumph was upon him, if he could only survive it.

    He drew deep from the power of the bond, pushing back against the corrupted ink flow. A great moan filled the air -- the collective cry of his people. Some swayed on their feet. The weakest collapsed. Many tried to run. And still deeper he pulled from their bloodline reservoir. But it wasn't enough to stop the ink tide.

    It was only when the breath trapped in his lungs began to fail him, and the inkpool darkness fully encased him, that he saw his way out. The bond he'd made with the Ashkavoran people -- his people -- meant to be drawn upon only in service of their protection... It flowed two ways.

    With a final surge of strength -- this time directed by more than just blind repulsion -- he pushed the ink torrent into the bond itself.

    Slowly, he felt the tide retreat... and heard the awful wails of his people as the corruption in turn rolled over them. When at last the ink relented, his eyes opened upon a world changed. The Ashkavoran people as he knew them were gone. Every last soul was transformed into terrifying shades of their former selves -- comprised no longer of blood and bone, only viscid, tainted ink.

    Leash Interactions
    The aforementioned sources cannot break the leash source, if cast before the leash debuff applied.
    • The leash mechanic has the following properties:
      • Checks the affected enemy unit's position and facing angle per server tick to the center of the leashed point, and sets the enemy's movement speed to a certain value when it is facing away from the leashed point center.
      • The set movement speed value are not displayed on the HUD and it reduces linearly toward the 700-break distance edges.
      • Teleport sources may break the leash.
      • Mechanic interactions with forced movement sources:
        • Can break the leash when forcefully moving beyond the leash radius, except Force Staff-based items.
        • Leash nullifies the forced movement source when the debuff expires while the forced movement source is happening.
        • However, Hurricane Thrust (Ally) may only break the leash if the affected target did not move after being leashed.
      • Both targets come to a full stop at when they are 600-distance apart.
      • Stretching both targets 700-distance apart breaks the leash, but does not dispel the debuff.
    • The debuff applies the following ability effects:
      • Leashes the primary target and the nearest allied enemy it is bound to.
      • Applies the following bonus vision effects over both targets:
        • Expose
        • Shared vision.
    • The search radius has the following properties:
      • The primary target can be an illusion or the Spirit Bear.
      • For the primary target:
        • Considers clones and creep-heroes as heroes, illusions as creeps.
      • Does not search for enemy heroes affected by the following sources:
      • Invisible units.
      • Invulnerable units.
    • Does not proc Spell Redirection in the following conditions:
      • Abilities cast by all creep-type units.
      • Item abilities cast by creep-heroes.
      • Abilities that do not proc Spell Reflection sources.
      • When an illusion is the primary target.
    • Most ability effects are first applied to the secondary target, then the primary target.
      • Charge-based abilities only consume a single charge when the ability is also cast on the secondary target.
    • Soulbind has the lowest priority of all Spell Reflection sources.
    • Certain abilities that do not affect debuff immune units will still be cast on the leashed debuff immune target, applying other of its ability effects, despite not affecting them.

    • The ability animations are based on and are affected by status resistance. This affects the following things:
      • EXPR
        StatusResistance1 > StatusResistance2
        • The leash disappears, and the primary target is no longer affected by Soulbind.
        • However, the secondary target is still affected by Soulbind's effects.
      • EXPR
        StatusResistance2 > StatusResistance1
        • The chain disappears, and the secondary target is no longer affected by Soulbind.
        • The primary target is still affected by Soulbind, and leashes the next valid enemy hero within the search radius.
    • The ability's leash mechanic has the following properties:
      • Sets the affected enemy unit's current movement speed as fixed value upon applying the leash debuff.
        • EXPR
          SetMS = CurrentMS
      • Soulbind applies a 4 set movement speed reduction to either units per 1-distance away between both units, up to a min 0.1-set movement speed value.
      • Hence, the ability formulas are defined as:
        • EXPR
          When 0 < |(PrimaryTargetPosition - SecondaryTargetPosition)| < 594.975, SetMS = 2380 - 4 × |(PrimaryTargetPosition - SecondaryTargetPosition)|
        • EXPR
          When 594.975 < |(PrimaryTargetPosition - SecondaryTargetPosition)| < 780, SetMS = 0.1
        • The set movement speed value along the edges does not decrease below the original movement speed.

    Trivia

    [edit]
    • Grimstroke was revealed during The International 2018, alongside with Mars in the same trailer.[1]
    • Grimstroke's abilities have different names in the game files:
      • Stroke of Fate: "Dark Artistry"
      • Phantom's Embrace: "Ink Creature"
      • Ink Swell: "Spirit Walk"
      • Soulbind: "Soul Chain"
    • ▶️ "More art than science." is a reference to the common phrase "More of an art than a science", when referring to an activity or field that is not bound by clearly-defined rules and is open to subjective interpretation.
    • ▶️ "At least you died with a smile on your face." is a reference to album of the label "Bella Union".
    • ▶️ "Welcome to the real void." perhaps a reference to the film "Matrix" - "Welcome to the real world".
    • After killing Lich he says ▶️ "Another self-styled king meets his end." perhaps a reference to the Lich-King in the fantasy world "Warcraft".
    • ▶️ "Brush beats scythe." is a reference to game "Rock, paper, scissors".
