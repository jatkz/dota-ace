    Slices through his enemies with precise strikes.
    What laid under the Juggernaut's mask, none could say. The last of his kind, Yurnero carries alone the martial traditions of his native isle, now sunken forever beneath the waves. Dancing with his Blade, the Juggernaut elevates swordplay to an art form, Swiftly Slashing foes in an unstoppable Blade Fury, striking the most vulnerable spots. With skills honed to such perfection, Yurnero can keep on Slashing and Dashing between opponents while giving them absolutely no chance to retaliate even in the slightest. Their only option to escape this onslaught is to book it as quickly as possible. After eviscerating his foes, Yurnero places his portable mobile Healing Ward that emits meditative energies to revitalize himself and nearby allies.

    Bio[edit]

    By the Visage of Vengeance, which drowned in the Isle of Masks, I will carry on the rites of the Faceless Ones.
    ▶️  Yurnero, the Juggernaut
    Alias:
    Yurnero
    Lore:
    No one has ever seen the face hidden beneath the mask of Yurnero the Juggernaut. It is only speculation that he even has one. For defying a corrupt lord, Yurnero was exiled from the ancient Isle of Masks--a punishment that saved his life. The isle soon after vanished beneath the waves in a night of vengeful magic. He alone remains to carry on the Isle's long Juggernaut tradition, one of ritual and swordplay. The last practitioner of the art, Yurnero's confidence and courage are the result of endless practice; his inventive bladework proves that he has never stopped challenging himself. Still, his motives are as unreadable as his expression. For a hero who has lost everything twice over, he fights as if victory is a foregone conclusion.
    Voice:
    David Scully (Responses)

    Bladestorm
    Blade Fury has a chance to deal additional magical spell damage per damage interval based on Blade Dance's critical strike values.

    This ability component levels up with Blade Dance..
    Proc Chance: 35%
    Attack Damage to Spell Damage Factor: 1.3/1.5/1.7/1.9 (1.42/1.62/1.82/2.02)
    • The ability has the following properties:
      • Cannot be cast while Omnislash-based abilities are active, and vice versa.
      • Does not interrupt Juggernaut's channeling abilities upon cast.
      • The damage interval checks for Juggernaut's current attack speed value per server tick and is defined as:
        • EXPR
          DamageInt = (100 / 1.4 / ΣAS ) / 2 , MAX DamageInt = 0.033
        • This immediately affects enemies upon applying the magicalspell damage per instance debuff.
    • The magicalspell damagedebuff applied has the following properties:
      • Reapplying the debuff does not update the damage values of the first applied debuff.
      • However, Blade Fury of different sources applies its debuff and damage values independently.
    • Grants the following bonuses upon cast:
      • Applies a basic dispel on Juggernaut.
      • Debuff immunity.
      • Bonus magic resistance.
    • Reduces Juggernaut's main and bonus attack damage upon his attack point, despite the reduction values not displayed on the HUD.
      • Does not affect bonus conditional attack damage sources.
      • Stacks additively with other total attack damage reduction sources.
      • Does not affect attacks on the following enemy units:
        • Buildings
        • Ward-type units.
    • Applies a strong dispel on Juggernaut at the end of the duration, including the following conditions:
      • While hidden.
      • Upon Juggernaut's death.
    • SHARD NOTES
      Creates a negative aura that applies the movement speed slow to enemy units within the radius upon cast.
    • FACET NOTES
      The Bladestorm  facet grants Blade Fury a chance to deal additional magical spell damage per damage interval while active.
      • The additional damage instance is applied with the damage per interval component as a single damage instance in the combat log.
      • Does not utilize the critical strike mechanic regardless of the ability visual effects.
      • Bladestorm uses pseudo-random distribution.
    • ABILITY DRAFT
      The main and bonus attack damage reduction component only affects Blade Fury's instant attack component.
      • Does not affect other attack modifiers or on-hit effects.
    modifier_juggernaut_blade_fury
    Blade Fury: Providing spell immunity and dealing damage every second
    modifier_jugg_caster
    Hidden modifier
    • The ability additionally has the following properties:
      • Can deal a max 30 damage instances per second, because the min damage interval cannot be lower than a server tick.
      • Has the following damage interval per second per current attack speed on Juggernaut:
        • 100/200/300/400/500 attack speed: 0.357/0.179/0.119/0.089/0.071-second damage interval.
    • Grants the following bonuses while active:
      • Has 85% magic resistance.
      • Blade Fury increases the total magic resistance by 0.2% per 10 intelligence points.
    • Ranged heroes use their attack projectiles for instant attacks, Blade Fury's attack damage reduction still applies to their attacks, causing their instant attacks to deal a 0-damage instance.
    • The ability effects are applied as follows:
      1. The magicalspell damage instances.
      2. The movement speed slow aura.

    Trivia[edit]

    • Juggernaut's backstory and appearance bear many similarities to that of the Seguleh people in the "Malazan Book of the Fallen" series of books by Steven Erikson.
    • Juggernaut's ultimate, Omnislash, is inspired by and named after the signature move of "Cloud Strife" from the game "Final Fantasy VII".[1]
    • ▶️ "Screw it, I'm going to five blades!" is a reference to an article from The Onion about the increasing number of blades put into razors.[2]
    • ▶️ "And then... there were none..." may be a reference to the Agatha Christie book by the same name.
    • ▶️ "I'm the Juggernaut, Lich!" is a play on the quote "I'm the Juggernaut, bitch.", taken from a YouTube parody featuring re-dubbed scenes from "X-Men: The Animated Series".
    • The Battle Banner of the Masked, Jagged Honor Banner, and Lineage of the Stormlords - Back banners are called Sashimono. These poles were attached to the backs of the armor (Dō) by special fittings. The Sashimono were worn both by foot soldiers as well as by the elite samurai and members of the shogunate. Like many heraldic displays, it served several functions in battle.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Juggernaut's hero concept is originally based on the Blademaster, an Orc melee hero.
        • Blade Fury's original iteration is identical to the Blademaster's Bladestorm.
        • Blade Dance's original iteration is identical to the Blademaster's Critical Strike.
      • Healing Ward's original iteration is identical to the Witch Doctors' Healing Ward.
