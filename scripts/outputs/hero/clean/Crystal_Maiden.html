    Bio

    [edit]
    When Hell freezes over, I'll start calling it Heaven.
    ▶️  Rylai, the Crystal Maiden
    Aliases:
    CM, Rylai, Wolf
    Lore:
    Born in a temperate realm, raised with her fiery older sister Lina, Rylai the Crystal Maiden soon found that her innate elemental affinity to ice created trouble for all those around her. Wellsprings and mountain rivers froze in moments if she stopped to rest nearby; ripening crops were bitten by frost, and fruiting orchards turned to mazes of ice and came crashing down, spoiled. When their exasperated parents packed Lina off to the equator, Rylai found herself banished to the cold northern realm of Icewrack, where she was taken in by an Ice Wizard who had carved himself a hermitage at the crown of the Blueheart Glacier. After long study, the wizard pronounced her ready for solitary practice and left her to take his place, descending into the glacier to hibernate for a thousand years. Her mastery of the Frozen Arts has only deepened since that time, and now her skills are unmatched.

    Trivia

    [edit]
    • Rylai is one of the oldest heroes from the original DotA in Reign of Chaos DotA. Her title used to be Frost Maiden.
    • Crystal Maiden is Lina's younger sister, with both sharing a bitter sibling-rivalry.
    • Her leveling-up response ▶️ "Is it cold in here or is it just me?" is a reference to the action movie Demolition Man.[1]
    • Her respawning line ▶️ "You only live ice..." is a reference to the 1967 James Bond film You Only Live Twice based on the novel of the same name.
    • Crystal Maiden's line ▶️ "It's all fun and games until someone's frozen solid." is a reference to a novel written by Christopher Brookmyre entitled All Fun and Games until Somebody Loses an Eye.
    • Crystal Maiden's rare response ▶️ "Can you guess my... favorite poet?" is a reference to Robert Frost due to his last name, and also an allusion to Frost's poem Fire and Ice.
      • She is the ice, and her sister Lina is fire. An excerpt: "Some say the world will end in fire, some say in ice ... know enough of hate, to say that for destruction ice is also great, and would suffice.".
    • Crystal Clone may be inspired by the Ice Klone special move of Sub-Zero from the Mortal Kombat franchise.
      • Sub-Zero and Crystal Maiden both jump back and leave an ice clone in their previous location.
      • Sub-Zero's clone freezes the enemy on contact, while Crystal Maiden's clone freezes enemies after taking enough damage.
    • In DotA, Frostbite could be cast on the enemy's Fountain, preventing it from attacknig.
      • Since the Fountain is a non-hero unit, it used the creep duration on it, which used to be 10 seconds.
    • During the Crownfall event, Crystal Maiden's full name was revealed to be "Rylai Moon".
    • In League of Legends, there is an item named Rylai's Crystal Scepter, as an homage to Crystal Maiden.
      • The item's theme follows Crystal Maiden's as well, applying a frost-themed effect.
      • The icon itself is similar to the icon of Brilliance Aura, which is used by Crystal Maiden's aura in DotA as well.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Crystal Maiden uses the model of Jaina Proudmoore, a campaign-only variant of the HumanArchmage hero.
        • In some very early versions of DotA, Rylai's title was once changed to "Batrider" and used Troll Batrider model.
        • Her frost and ice theme may be inspired by the Archmage's Blizzard ability.
        • Arcane Aura's original iteration was identical to the Archmage's Brilliance Aura.
          • In DotA, the aura was named Brilliance Aura as well. It got changed to Arcane Aura during early beta in Dota 2.
        • Freezing Field is inspred by and based on the Archmage's Blizzard.
      • Frostbite's original iteration was identical to the Keeper of the Grove's Entangle.
      • Crystal Nova is based on and inspired by the Lich's Frost Nova.
      • Its predecessor, Frost Nova, was identical to Lich's Frost Nova.
      • In DotA, Crystal Maiden's full name is Lyrai Crestfall. The surname was dropped to avoid copyright issues with "Blizzard Entertainment", since Crestfall is a location within the "Warcraft" universe.
        • Conversely, a human mage NPC with the name Lyrai Crestfall was added to World of Warcraft, as an homage to the Crystal Maiden.
