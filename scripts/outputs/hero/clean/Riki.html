    Bio

    [edit]
    There are none so stabbed as those who will not see.
    ▶️  Riki, the Stealth Assassin
    Aliases:
    SA, Stealth Assassin
    Lore:
    Riki was born middle child to the great dynasty of Tahlin. With an older brother groomed for the throne, and a younger brother coddled and kept, Riki, the small middle son, seemed born for the art of invisibility. It was an art he cultivated, and one which ultimately saved his life on the night that his people were betrayed and his family slaughtered. Of all the royal line, he alone escaped—small and agile, unassuming, using smoke as cover. He cut his way out of the royal grounds, using the advantage of surprise, quietly slitting the throats of one enemy warrior after another. Now free of his royal responsibilities, Riki uses his talents in service to a new trade: Stealth Assassin. He silences his enemies, sharpening his skills, hoping to one day take revenge on those who killed his family and robbed him of his birthright.

    Trivia

    [edit]
    • ▶️"Good assassins never die… they just fade away.", is a reference to a quote by General Douglas MacArthur, "Old soldiers never die; they just fade away."
    • ▶️ "There are none so stabbed as those who will not see." is a reference to a proverb: "There are none so blind as those who will not see."
    • ▶️ "I speak softly, but carry a sharp knife." is a reference to the West African Proverb "Speak softly and carry a big stick.", popularized by U.S. president Theodore Roosevelt.
    • Riki's model is based on the Satyr, a goat-like humanoid creature from greek mythology.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Riki uses the model of the Satyr Trickster, a small neutral creep.
      • Smoke Screen is inspired by and based on the Dragonhawk Rider's Cloud.
      • Backstab's original iteration was a basic Critical Strike, identical to the Blademaster's Critical Strike.
      • Blink Strike's original iteration was a basic Blink, identical to the Warden's Blink.
      • Permanent Invisibility's original iteration was a identical to the Shadow Wolves' Permanent Invisibility.
      • In DotA, Riki's name is "Rikimaru" (力丸), a reference to one of the main characters in the Tenchu video game series.
        • On the Perfect World client, Riki still is named Rikimaru.
      • Riki has a 1/10 chance to spawn in with the name "Riki Martin", which is a reference to the Latino pop singer Ricky Martin.
