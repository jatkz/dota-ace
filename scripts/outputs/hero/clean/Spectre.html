    Haunts her enemies and teleports to her target.
    An eternal being of violent energies, Mercurial, the Spectre is irresistibly drawn to conflict. Throwing out a Spectral Dagger, creating a shadowy trail to her foes in which she may travel unobstructed. Harm done to Mercurial is partially deflected and Dispersed onto nearby foes that prolongs her endurance. Even in the most Desolate wilderness, travelers are Haunted by visions of Spectre, seeming to prey on their solitude. But these are no mere illusions. It is said that for one in every five haunted, the apparition becomes Reality, and alongside these unfortunate victims the Spectre Shadow Steps to, bringing with her the destruction and wrath that she eternally craves.

    Bio

    [edit]
    Among the Transcendencies, you would be thought ignoble.
    ▶️  Mercurial, the Spectre
    Alias:
    Mercurial
    Lore:
    Just as higher states of energy seek a lower level, the Spectre known as Mercurial is a being of intense and violent energy who finds herself irresistibly drawn to scenes of strife as they unfold in the physical world. While her normal spectral state transcends sensory limitations, each time she takes on a physical manifestation, she is stricken by a loss of self--though not of purpose. In the clash of combat, her identity shatters and reconfigures, and she begins to regain awareness. She grasps that she is Mercurial the Spectre--and that all of her Haunts are but shadows of the one true Spectre. Focus comes in the struggle for survival; her true mind reasserts itself; until in the final moments of victory or defeat, she transcends matter and is restored once more to her eternal form.

    Gin Hammond (Responses)

    • The ability effects are applied as follows:
        • Reflects all incoming damage (before reductions), with the reflected damage value (RDmg) defined as:
          • EXPR
            RDmg = IncomingDmg × 1.5 × 8%/12%/16%/20% OR 12%/16%/20%/24%
            • Damage taken by Spectre can only be affected by other offensive incoming damage manipulation sources applied on Spectre.
            • Hence the ability fully reflects overkill damage dealt to Spectre.
          • Does not reflect HP Removal sources.
      2. Applies RDmg to all enemy units within radius with the following properties:
        • The reflected damage is applied as the same damage type applied to Spectre from the damage source.
        • Has the HP Removal flag, and does not proc on-damage effects.
          • The closer the proximity to Spectre, the greater the RDmg applied to the enemy units.
            • Increases by 1.78%/2.67%/3.56%/4.44% (2.67%/3.56%/4.44%/5.33%) per 100-distance toward the min radius.
        • Spectre receives the damage with a generic incoming damage reduction applied.
          • Hence, if both Spectre and an enemy would die to the damage simultaneously, the enemy is always killed first.
        • The generic incoming damage has the following properties, and is defined as:
          • EXPR
            DamageReceived = IncomingDmg × 1.5 × 8%/12%/16%/20% OR 12%/16%/20%/24%
            • Affects all damage types.
            • Stacks additively with other generic incoming damage sources.
        • Does not reduce damage of HP Removal sources.

      Adds an active component to Dispersion that increases the damage reduction and damage reflection values upon cast.
        • Interrupts Spectre's channeling abilities upon cast.
        • Since break sources fully disables the ability, the active ability becomes non-functional and does nothing upon cast.

    Trivia

    [edit]
    • The word "Mercurial" comes from latin and describes a state of rapid and unpredictable change.
    • Warcraft 3 similarities:
      • Spectre uses the model of the Avatar of Vengeance, a summon of the Night ElfWarden hero.
        • On release, Spectre's title was Avatar of Vengeance as well. The title was changed 6.33, along with a full hero rework.
        • Spectre's name, Mercurial, is in honour of the artist who created the loading screen that was used from versions 6.28-6.32.[1]
        • In DotA, Spectre uses the voice of the Spirit of Vengeance, which are reversed and heavily filtered voice lines of the Warden.
        • This was the inspiration for Spectre's voice filters in Dota 2, which are heavily filtered, and have a reversed instasnce overlayed.
      • Spectre has a 1/12 chance to spawn in with the name "Qwerty-", as tribute to the professional DotA player, who died on december 5, 2010.
