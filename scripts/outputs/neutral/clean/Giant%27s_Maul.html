    Crushing Blow[edit]

    Crushing Blow
    ▶️
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Enemies
    Empowers the next attack with a critical hit, and slows the target's movement, attack, and cast speed for a short while.
    Critical Damage: 150%
    Move Speed Slow: 10%
    Attack Speed Slow: 15%
    Cast Speed Slow: 20%

    When crafted with the Dormant Curio, the critical damage is increased by 30%.

    • Details
    • Status Effects
    • Show All
    • When another source of critical strike procs on the same attack, the higher damage value takes priority.
      • The other effects of Crushing Blow are still applied normally.
    • Crushing Blow triggers upon executing an attack, regardless of its outcome.
      • For ranged heroes, this is upon projectile launch.
      • However, the effects are only applied and the cooldown only begins if the attack lands on the target.
      • This means if multiple attack projectiles are launched, only the first launched one can apply the attack.
      • Even if the first launched one misses or is disjointed, other already launched attack projectiles cannot apply the effects.
    • The attack speed slow is a percentage, applied to the total attack speed.
