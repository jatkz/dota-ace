    Ability[edit]

    Relentless[edit]

    Relentless
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    Increases slow resistance. This bonus is decreased for every enemy hero within close range.
    Enemy Search Radius: 600
    Max Slow Resistance: 100%
    Slow Resistance Loss per Enemy: 20%
    Increases status resistance for each enemy within the same area.
    Status Resistance Bonus per Enemy: 10%
    When crafted with the Dormant Curio, the max slow resistance is increased by 30%.
    Max Slow Resistance: 130%
    Details
    • Does not react on Invisible heroes or on heroes in the Fog of War
    • Treats Creep-Heroes and Illusions as heroes.
    • The Slow Resistance and Status Resistance scale as follows:
      • 0 Heroes = 100% ( 100%) Slow Resistance, 0% Status Resistance
      • 1 Hero = 80% ( 100%) Slow Resistance, 10% Status Resistance
      • 2 Heroes = 60% ( 90%) Slow Resistance, 20% Status Resistance
      • 3 Heroes = 40% ( 70%) Slow Resistance, 30% Status Resistance
      • 4 Heroes = 20% ( 50%) Slow Resistance, 40% Status Resistance
      • 5 Heroes = 0% ( 30%) Slow Resistance, 50% Status Resistance
      • 6 Heroes = 0% ( 10%) Slow Resistance, 60% Status Resistance
      • 7 Heroes = 0% ( 0%) Slow Resistance, 70% Status Resistance
      • The status resistance bonus is not capped and can increase further.
