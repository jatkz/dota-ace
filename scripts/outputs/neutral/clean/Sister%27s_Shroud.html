    Abilities[edit]

    Veiled[edit]

    Veiled
    ▶️
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    Upon falling below the health threshold, grants the wielder enormous amounts of evasion for a short while.
    Health Threshold: 50%
    Evasion Bonus: 200%
    Duration: 8
    While the buff is active, whenever the wielder evades an attack, the evasion bonus is reduced based on the current bonus.
    Current Evasion Bonus Reduction: 75%
    8
    • Details
    • Status Effects
    • Show All
    • Triggers upon taking damage from an enemy while below 50% health.
      • triggers on any damage, not just attacks.
    • The buff is applied after the triggering damage instance.
      • This means if triggered by an attack, that attack is not evaded
    • Once triggered, each attack that misses the wielder reduces the evasion bonus by 75% of the current value.
      • Grants 200%/50%/12.5%/3.13%/0.78% evasion after evading 0/1/2/3/4 attacks.
    • The evasion uses pseudo-random distribution.
      • Stacks diminishingly with other evasion sources.
