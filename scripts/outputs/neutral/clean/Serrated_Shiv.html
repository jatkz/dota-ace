    Gut 'Em[edit]

    Gut 'Em
    ▶️
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Enemies
    Attacks have a chance to have True Strike and deal a percentage of the target's current health as bonus physical damage.
    Proc Chance: 20%
    Current Health as Attack Damage Bonus: 8%
    Deals a flat attack damage bonus against Roshan.
    Attack Damage Bonus: 200
    When crafted with the Dormant Curio, the current health as attack damage bonus and the attack damage bonus against Roshan are increased by 30%.
    Current Health as Attack Damage Bonus: 10.4%
    Attack Damage Bonus Against Roshan: 260
    1
    Details
    • Serrated Shiv uses pseudo-random distribution.
      • Cannot proc against Buildings.
      • Can proc against, but does not apply the attack damage bonus against them.
      • Fully works against ward-type units.
    • The effects are calculated upon reaching the attack point, not upon landing an attack.
      • This means the ability can proc on missed attacks.
      • For ranged heroes, this is upon projectile launch.
