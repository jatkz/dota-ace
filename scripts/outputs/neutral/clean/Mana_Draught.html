    Abilities[edit]

    Bottoms Up[edit]

    Bottoms Up
    ▶️
    Ability
    No / Unit
    Affects
    Self / Allied Heroes
    Restores the user's mana over the duration by increasing their mana regeneration, based on a fixed amount plus a portion of their maximum mana.
    Cast Animation: 0 + 0
    Cast Range:  650
    Flat Total Mana Restore: 70
    Max Mana Restore: 3%
    Duration: 6
    When crafted with the Dormant Curio, the max mana restore is increased by 30%.
    Max Mana Restore: 3.9%
    Taking more than 0 damage from any other player (after reductions and not flagged as HP Removal) dispels the buff.
    Taking more than 0 damage from Roshan (after reductions) dispels the buff.

    Fill Up[edit]

    Fill Up
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self / Allied Heroes
    The cooldown speed of Bottoms Up is increased while standing in waters.
    Cooldown Reduction: 30%
    Details
    • The cooldown updates immediately upon leaving or entering any body of water.
    • The reduction is always based on the current remaining cooldown.
      • This means when entering waters while e.g. 10 seconds are left on the cooldown, it drops down to 7 seconds.
