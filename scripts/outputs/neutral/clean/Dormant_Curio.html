    Hidden Potential[edit]

    Hidden Potential
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    Neutral items crafted while holding the Dormant Curio have their potency increased considerably.
    Potency Increase: 30%
    Details
    • Directly buffs the next neutral item's ability stats.
      • See the individual neutral items' articles for details.
    • The effect is provided by the Unleashed Enchantment that is built in to this artifact.
      • However, Techies' Spoon's Stash facet does not keep this enchantment.
