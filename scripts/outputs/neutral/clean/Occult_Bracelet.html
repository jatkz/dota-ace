    Rites of Eloshar[edit]

    Rites of Eloshar
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    Each time the wearer is attacked they gain a stack of 0.4 mana regen, up to 5 stacks. Stacks last for 5 seconds.
    Max Stacks: 5
    Mana Regen Bonus per Stack: 0.4
    Duration: 5
    • Details
    • Status Effects
    • Show All
    • Rites of Eloshar can give at most 2 mana regeneration bonus.
      • With max stacks and its duration, it can regenerate up to a total of 10 mana.
    • Each stack places a new hidden modifier buff on its wielder. This means their durations are independent from each other.
      • The number of stacks can be seen on the status buff icon.
