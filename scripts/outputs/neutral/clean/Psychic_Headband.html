    • Show All
    • With the item's cast range bonus, Psychic Push has a total cast range of 400.
    • Pushes the target at a speed of 1333.33 ( 1733.33).
    • The target is not disabled during the push, so they still can cast abilities, attack, and turn during the push.
      • Does not interrupt channeling abilities upon cast.
    • Does not push units affected by Black Hole, Chronosphere, Duel or Flaming Lasso.
    • Cannot push units through Kinetic Field or Arena of Blood.
    • If the target is affected by Soulbind, it cannot push the unit further than its max stretch distance allows.
    • If the target is leashed by Pounce, the leash gets instantly broken upon cast.
      • However, if the target gets leashed after cast, it cannot push the unit further than the max stretch distance of Pounce allows.
    • Cannot be cast on wards or buildings. Can be cast on Homing Missile.
    • Can move units over impassable terrain. Trees within 200 radius around the pushed target gets destroyed during the push.
    • Can be cast on Roshan but without any effect, since Roshan is immune to forced movement.
    • Does not share cooldown with Force Staff-based items.
    Interactions: Debuff Immunity
    The forced movement is instantly canceled.
