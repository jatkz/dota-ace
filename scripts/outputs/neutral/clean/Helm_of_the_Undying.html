    Death Delay[edit]

    Death Delay
    ▶️
    Ability
    Passive
    Affects
    Self
    When receiving a killing blow, the death gets delayed for a short time, during which the wielder can still act freely.
    Base Duration: 5
    While active, enemy heroes that die nearby extend the duration.
    Death Radius: 1200

    • Misc.
    • Show All
    • The wielder enters Death Delay when their health reaches 1, unless they are affected by Shallow Grave, Battle Trance, Wraith Delay, or have Reincarnation.
      • The wielder is kept alive by having their minimum health set to 1. Damage taken is technically not reduced.
      • Death Delay does not trigger for Meepo if one of his Meepo Clones get killed, instead of the prime Meepo who carries the item.
    • Death Delay grants the wielder unobstructed movement, allowing them to move through units, trees, and over cliffs.
    • During Death Delay, the wielder is still alive, so they are still fully targetable and affected by spells and attacks.
      • Since the wielder did not die yet, buffs and debuffs are not dispelled either, like how they would upon death.
    • When Death Delay expires, the wielder dies. The kill is credited to whoever triggered it.
      • The wielder cannot be saved by any means. They will die once the effect expires, even if they are invulnerable, or anything that usually prevents death.
      • If the wielder gains reincarnation during Death Delay, it still triggers regularly and reincarnates the wielder.
    • Death Delay causes the wielder's model size to increase by 25% for its duration. This has no impact on their collision size.
